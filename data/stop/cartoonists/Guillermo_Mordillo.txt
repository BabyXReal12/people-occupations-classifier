spanish parents mordillo spent childhood villa pueyrredón buenos aires early interest drawing 1948 obtained certificate illustrator school journalism years later continuing study part animation team burone bruch illustrated childrens stories tales perrault tales schmid musicians bremen three little pigs edited codex 1952 cofounded galas studios dedicated production animations meanwhile continued develop career illustrator published strips local magazines

november 1955 moved lima peru worked freelance designer advertising company mccann erickson 1958 aesops fables illustrated samaniego editorial iberia lima

done greetingcard illustrations kansas citybased hallmark cards left united states 1960 employed paramount pictures studios york part performance film globally significant characters popeye little lulu creates characters short film trick tree

three years later august life changed direction time travelled europe arriving paris september worked first humorous letters micmax edition july 1966 began collaborate magazine pelerin shortly paris match years later work started printed publications parts world notably germanys stern paris wife amparo camarasa marrying 1969 children sebastian jerome 1970 cecile isabelle 1972

1980 moved mallorca spain named president international association authors comics cartoons cfia based geneva switzerland years spain returned france 1998 following year participates creative workshop zermatt switzerland

2007 leaves drawings color experiment materials acrylics pastels crayons following year performed petrus series reproductions pictures high definition also year sold internet publication numbered signed author mordillo collection

currently resides monaco
