
John Astle (born March 31, 1943) is an American politician from Maryland and a member of the Democratic Party (United States) Democratic Party. He is currently serving in his 4th term in the Maryland State Senate, representing Marylands District 30 in Anne Arundel County, Maryland Anne Arundel County.<ref name="mdmanual">{{cite web title=John C. Astle, Maryland State Senator url=http://msa.maryland.gov/msa/mdmanual/05sen/html/msa12160.html website=Maryland Manual On-Line publisher=Maryland State Archives accessdate=January 17, 2017}}</ref>

==Background==
Astle was born in Charles Town, West Virginia and grew up in Barboursville, West Virginia Barboursville. He graduated in 1966 from Marshall University in Huntington, West Virginia and attended the Catholic University of America.<ref name="mdmanual" />

==Military service &mdash; United States Marine Corps==
He joined the United States Marine Corps Reserve in 1961 and received a commission as an officer in the Marine Corps through its Officer Candidates School (United States Marine Corps) Platoon Leaders Class program upon graduation from Marshall. He was a Naval Aviator flying helicopters and left active duty with the rank of Captain. He also flew the Presidential helicopter for three and a half years.<ref name="mdmanual" />

He retired from the Marine Corps Reserves as a Colonel after 30 years of service.<ref name="mdmanual" />  He saw combat in Vietnam, earning 34 Air Medals and 2 Purple Hearts. He recalled to active duty for Desert Storm.

==Political career==

Astle was originally elected to the Maryland House of Delegates in 1982. He was elected to the State Senate in 1994 and currently serves as Vice-Chair of the Senate Finance Committee.<ref name="20170117-story">{{cite news last1=Cook first1=Chase title=Annapolis senator John Astle to run for mayor url=http://www.capitalgazette.com/news/elections/ph-ac-cn-astle-for-mayor-0118-20170117-story.html accessdate=January 17, 2017 work=The Capital date=January 17, 2017}}</ref>
