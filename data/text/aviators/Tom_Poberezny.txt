Thomas Paul "Tom" Poberezny (born October 3, 1946) is a former aerobatic world champion, as well as chairman of the annual Experimental Aircraft Association (EAA) EAA AirVenture Oshkosh Fly-In and Convention from 1977 to 2011 and president of EAA from 1989 to 2010. He succeeded his father, Paul Poberezny, who founded the organization in 1953.
He is also one of the founders of the Young Eagles, an EAA program created to give children the opportunity to experience flight and to learn about general aviation.
