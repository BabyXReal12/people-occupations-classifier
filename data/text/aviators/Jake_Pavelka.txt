
Jacob Lynn "Jake" Pavelka (born January 27, 1978) is an United States American pilot who appeared on six reality TV programs, most notably The Bachelor (US TV series) The Bachelor.

== Early life and career ==
Pavelka was born in Dallas, Texas to Sallie Lanell (née Mack) and James Lynn Pavelka. He is the middle child of three sons. His brothers, Jason and Matthew, are both married. He has two nephews. Pavelka grew up in Denton, Texas and he started taking flying lessons when he was twelve. He eventually worked as a pilot for Atlantic Southeast Airlines, and under the name of Jake Landrum, acted in several television commercials and programs produced in the Dallas area.<ref name=TVG>{{cite news   author =   title =Jake Pavelka Biography   publisher =TV Guide Online    date =   url =http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/jake-pavelka/bio/298929   accessdate=2009-03-30}}</ref> Pavelka is a cousin of fitness model, television presenter, actor and personal trainer Jessie Pavelka.
Pavelka participates in triathlons and has been active in fitness throughout his life.

== Reality television ==

Pavelka first appeared as a contestant in 2009, in the fifth season of the American Broadcasting Company ABC reality show The Bachelorette. He was eliminated from the competition, but was brought back by the network to star in the The Bachelor (season 14) 14th season of its series The Bachelor in 2010. The program’s run ended with Pavelka proposing marriage to contestant Vienna Girardi in the March 2010 finale.<ref name=TVG /> The couple split up in June 2010.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.tvguide.com/News/Jake-Vienna-Split-1019818.aspx title=The Bachelors Jake Pavelka and Vienna Girardi Split publisher=TVGuide.com}}</ref>

Following the end of The Bachelor, Pavelka was named as one of the celebrity contestants for the tenth season of Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 10) Dancing with the Stars.<ref>{{cite news   author =Joyce Eng   title =Dancing’s Jake Pavelka: I Feel Dumb Every Day in Rehearsal   publisher =Seattle Post-Intelligencer    date =March 25, 2010   url =http://www.seattlepi.com/tvguide/417403_dancing25.html  accessdate=2010-03-30}} {{Dead link date=October 2010 bot=H3llBot}}</ref> He and his partner Chelsie Hightower were the fifth to be eliminated, in April 2010. In July 2011, Pavelka was a contestant on the second season of Bachelor Pad.<ref>{{cite web last=Bierly  first=Mandi  url=http://insidetv.ew.com/2011/06/13/bachelor-pad-cast-season-2/  title=Official Bachelor Pad cast revealed - EXCLUSIVE &#124; Inside TV &#124; EW.com  publisher=Insidetv.ew.com  date=2011-06-13  accessdate=2012-07-26}}</ref> He was also a contestant on VH1s 2011 reality series Famous Food, and appeared on a September 2011 episode of The CWs reality show H8R.  In 2014, he was a contestant on Rachael vs. Guy: Celebrity Cook-Off.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.foodnetwork.com/rachael-vs-guy-celebrity-cook-off/index.html title=Rachael vs. Guy: Celebrity Cook-Off publisher= accessdate=8 August 2018}}</ref> On May 23, 2016, he appeared as a surprise guest on the Bachelorette with JoJo Fletcher.
