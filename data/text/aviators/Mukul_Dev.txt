
Mukul Dev is an Indian television and film actor known for his Hindi films and TV serials and music albums. He has also acted in few Tamil language Tamil , Malayalam, Kannada language Kannada and Telugu cinema Telugu films.<ref>{{cite news url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-04-21/news-interviews/29458871_1_hindi-film-mukul-dev-share-screen-space title=Mukul Devs dozen films in two years newspaper=The Times of India first=Aparna last=Phadke accessdate=19 October 2011 date=21 April 2011 archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140907173904/http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-04-21/news-interviews/29458871_1_hindi-film-mukul-dev-share-screen-space deadurl=no archivedate=7 September 2014 df=dmy-all}}</ref>
. He received the 7th Amrish Puri Award, for excellence in acting for his role in Yamla Pagla Deewana.<ref>{{cite web url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/hindi/bollywood/news/Mukul-Dev-gets-Amrish-Puri-Award-for-Yamla-Pagla-Deewana/articleshow/9324363.cms title=Mukul Dev gets Amrish Puri Award for Yamla Pagla Deewana newspaper=The Times of India first=Suruchi last=Sharma date=22 July 2011 accessdate=19 October 2011}}</ref>

He is also a trained pilot from the Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Uran Akademi.<ref>{{cite news url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-01-18/news-interviews/28377623_1_rahul-dev-mukul-dev-shooting first=Diksha last=Kamra title=Mukul Dev is back in action newspaper=The Times of India accessdate=19 October 2011 date=18 January 2011 archivedate=7 September 2014 archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140907174332/http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2011-01-18/news-interviews/28377623_1_rahul-dev-mukul-dev-shooting deadurl=no df=dmy-all}}</ref>

==Career==
Mukul Dev made his acting debut on TV through a serial Mumkin playing role of Vijay Pandey in 1996. He also acted in Doordarshans Ek Se Badh Kar Ek, a comedy Bollywood countdown show.

He got his break in films through Dastak (1996 film) Dastak as ACP Rohit Malhotra in the same year which also introduced crowned Miss Universe Sushmita Sen. He appeared in several films such as Qila (film) Qila (1998), Wajood (1998 film) Wajood (1998), Kohram (1999) and Mujhe Meri Biwi Se Bachaao (2001). He acted in several television serials in the 2000s such as Kahin Diyaa Jale Kahin Jiyaa (2001), Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii (2003), Pyar Zindagi Hai (2003) and in a dance competition show Kabhi Kabhii Pyaar Kabhi Kabhii Yaar (2008). In recent years he has played supporting roles in films like Yamla Pagla Deewana (2011), Son of Sardaar (2012), R... Rajkumar (2013) and Jai Ho (film) Jai Ho (2014).

He was also the host of Fear Factor India of Season 1.<ref>{{cite web url=http://in.news.yahoo.com/mukul-dev-jat-buffalo-mission-070209721.html title=Mukul Dev - the Jat on a buffalo mission! work=Yahoo!
