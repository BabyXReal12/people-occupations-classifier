Timothy Simons (born June 12, 1978) is an American actor and comedian best known for his role as Jonah Ryan on the HBO television series Veep,
for which he has received three nominations for the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series.
He has also had acting roles in the films The Interview, Christine (2016 film) Christine, and The Boss (2016 film) The Boss.

==Personal life==
He was born in Readfield, Maine, the son of Susan and Ron Simons, and was raised in the state.
Simons is married to Annie Simons and they have twins born in December 2011.
He currently resides in Los Angeles.
