Mukesh Tiwari
(born 24 August 1965) is an Indian film actor known for playing negative and comic roles in Bollywood
and Cinema of India several other Indian language films.
he began his acting career with the Hindi film China Gate (1998 film) China Gate, in which he played the role of Jageera.

== Early life ==
Tiwari attended the National School of Drama (NSD) in 1994.
