Joey Bragg (born July 20, 1996)
<!-- NOTE: Source only confirms day-of-birth, not a year or an age... -->
is an American actor and comedian. He is known for his role as Liv and Maddie#Joey Rooney Joey Rooney in the Disney Channel sitcom Liv and Maddie, which aired from 2013 to 2017. He also played Peter Magoo in the 2012 television film Fred 3: Camp Fred, and co-headlined the 2015 Disney XD television film Mark &amp; Russells Wild Ride.
Bragg has a successful stand-up comedy career.
He is Jewish.
