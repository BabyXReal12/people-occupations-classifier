Joey Bragg (born July 20, 1996)<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.facebook.com/radiodisney/posts/10155906268270360:0 title=Radio Disney website=www.facebook.com accessdate=15 September 2017}}</ref><!-- NOTE: Source only confirms day-of-birth, not a year or an age... --> is an American actor and comedian. He is known for his role as Liv and Maddie#Joey Rooney Joey Rooney in the Disney Channel sitcom Liv and Maddie, which aired from 2013 to 2017.
