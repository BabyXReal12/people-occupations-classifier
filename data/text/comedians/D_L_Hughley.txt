Darryl Lynn "D. L." Hughley ({{IPAc-en ˈ h juː ɡ l iː}}; born March 6, 1963) is an American actor, political commentator, radio host, author and stand-up comedy stand-up comedian. Hughley is best known as the original host of BETs ComicView from 1992-1993, the eponymous character on the American Broadcasting Company ABC/UPN sitcom The Hughleys and performed in The Original Kings of Comedy. Additionally, he has been the host of CNNs D. L. Hughley Breaks the News, a correspondent for The Jay Leno Show on NBC, and a local radio personality and interviewer in New York City. In early 2013, D.L. Hughley landed in 9th place on Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 16) Dancing with the Stars.

==Early life==
Hughley was born in Los Angeles, California Los Angeles, California, the son of Audrey and Charles Hughley, who was a Delta Air Lines maintenance worker. During his troubled teenage years, Hughley was a member of the Bloods and was expelled from high school before turning his back on gang life, getting his GED, and obtaining employment with The Los Angeles Times.<ref>{{cite web

==Career==
From 1992 to 1993, Hughley was the original host of ComicView, the stand-up comedy program on Black Entertainment Television BET. Hughley: Notes From The GED Section" <ref>[http://www.comedycentral.com/press/press_releases/2005/061305_dlhughleycd.jhtml COMEDY CENTRAL® RECORDS RELEASES "D.L. ===Radio===
Hughley also has a career as an on-air radio personality.  On July 20, 2009, The D.L. Hughley Morning Show premiered on WRKS (now WEPN-FM WEPN), more popularly known at the time as 98.7 Kiss FM, an urban adult contemporary station in New York City.  His co-hosts included former Black Entertainment Television BET news correspondent Jacque Reid.<ref>{{cite news  url=http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/tv/2009/07/15/2009-07-15_dl_hughley_to_host_morning_show_on_new_yorks_kissfm_radio.html  title=D.L. Hughley to host morning show on New Yorks Kiss-FM radio  accessdate=2009-07-20  date=2009-07-15  publisher=New York Daily News   first=David   last=Hinckley}}</ref>  Airing from 6–10 am, the show placed Hughley in direct competition with his fellow "King of Comedy" Steve Harvey, whose nationally syndicated The Steve Harvey Morning Show Steve Harvey Morning Show airs in New York on WBLS (Kiss FMs longtime rival).  There were plans to take Hughleys show into syndication as well, but a dispute regarding his salary erupted between Kiss FMs parent company and the potential syndication company.  In August 2010, Kiss FM dropped the show from its schedule, and Hughley moved on to other endeavors.<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.radio-info.com/news/dl-hughley-morning-show-officially-off-wrks-new-york  title=D.L. Hughley Morning Show officially off WRKS New York  accessdate=2010-08-31  date=2010-08-18  publisher=Radio-Info.com  first=  last=  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100822194010/http://www.radio-info.com/news/dl-hughley-morning-show-officially-off-wrks-new-york  archivedate=2010-08-22  df= }}</ref>

On August 12, 2013, REACH Media, the syndicator founded by Tom Joyner, announced it had finalized a deal with D.L. to host a new nationally syndicated afternoon drive show, The D.L. Hughley Show, distributed by Cumulus Media Networks.<ref>{{cite web author=Lance Venta  url=http://radioinsight.com/blog/headlines/84331/d-l-hughley-to-host-syndicated-afternoon-show/  title=RadioInsight — D.L. ==Personal life==
Hughley and his wife, LaDonna, have two daughters, Ryan and Tyler, and a son, Kyle.<ref>[http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/dl-hughley/bio/145708 D.L. Hughley: Biography]. Tvguide.com. Retrieved on 2013-01-01.</ref> Hughley has discussed his son Kyles Asperger syndrome on several occasions.<ref>
[http://transcripts.cnn.com/TRANSCRIPTS/0901/10/dlh.01.html Hughley Discusses Blagojevich and Burris With Congressman Bobby Rush]. Transcripts.cnn.com (2009-01-10). Retrieved on 2013-01-01.</ref>

On November 10, 2017, D.L. Hughley gave an interview on Angela Yees Lipservice podcast in which he described having an affair early in his marriage and career. His mistress had become pregnant and had D.L.’s son; the infant was shaken to death by his mother’s boyfriend.<ref>[https://www.bet.com/celebrities/news/2017/11/10/dl-hughley.html BET]</ref>
