Melissa Ivy Rauch  (born June 23, 1980)<ref name=tvg /><!--cited source, TV Guide, also verified full name Melissa Ivy Rauch--> is an American actress and comedian. She is known for playing List of The Big Bang Theory characters#Bernadette Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz on the CBS sitcom The Big Bang Theory.

==Career==
Some of Rauchs early work was as a regular contributor to VH1s Best Week Ever television show.<ref>{{cite web  date=May 29, 2008  url=http://www.bestweekever.tv/tag/melissa-rauch   title=Profiles in Panelism: Melissa Rauch  work=Best Week Ever}}</ref>

In 2009, Rauch began playing the recurring role of Bernadette Rostenkowski, a co-worker of Penny (The Big Bang Theory) Penny who began to date Howard Wolowitz, in the third season of TVs The Big Bang Theory.<ref>{{cite journal  url=http://www.backstage.com/interview/the-big-bang-theory-cast-on-being-part-of-an-ensemble/  title=The Big Bang Theory Cast On Being Part of an Ensemble  first=Jenelle  last=Riley  work=Backstage (magazine) Backstage  date=November 29, 2012  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref> The following season her character became Howards fiancée and Rauch was promoted to a series regular.  The characters married in the season five finale. In December 2011, Rauch and fellow cast members of The Big Bang Theory received the first of four nominations for the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series.<ref name=18Nom>{{cite press release  title=Nominations Announced for the 18th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards®  url=http://www.sagawards.org/media-pr/11213  date=December 14, 2011  publisher=Screen Actors Guild  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name=19Nom>{{cite press release  title=Nominations Announced for the 19th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards®  url=http://www.sagawards.org/media-pr/press-releases/nominations-announced-19th-annual-screen-actors-guild-awards%C2%AE  date=December 12, 2012  publisher=Screen Actors Guild  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name=20Nom>{{cite press release  title=Nominations Announced for the 20th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards®  url=http://www.sagawards.org/media-pr/press-releases/nominations-announced-20th-annual-screen-actors-guild-awards%C2%AE  date=December 13, 2013  publisher=Screen Actors Guild  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref><ref name=E!>{{cite news  title=SAG Awards 2015: Complete List of Nominations!  url=http://www.eonline.com/news/604452/sag-awards-2015-complete-list-of-nominations  date=December 10, 2014  work=E! News  last=Johnson  first=Zach  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref>

Other acting credits include True Blood (on which she had a recurring role in 2010 as List of True Blood characters#Summer Summer, a girl who likes List of True Blood characters#Hoyt Hoyt), The Office (U.S. TV series) The Office, the Kath & Kim (U.S. TV series) American remake of the Australian TV series Kath & Kim, Wright v Wrong, and the film I Love You, Man.

Rauch is also part of the cast of the comedic stage show The Realest Real Housewives with Casey Wilson, June Diane Raphael, Jessica St. Clair and Danielle Schneider. The show began running at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in 2011.<ref name=Weekly>{{cite journal  url=http://www.laweekly.com/arts/the-realest-real-housewives-at-ucb-a-celebration-of-the-best-show-ever-paul-scheer-2374472  title=The Realest Real Housewives at UCB: A Celebration of the Best Show Ever + Paul Scheer  last=Trachta  first=Ali  date=January 29, 2011  work=LA Weekly  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref>

Rauch co-wrote and co-produced the film The Bronze (film) The Bronze, in which she starred as Hope Annabelle Greggory, a bronze medal-winning Gymnastics gymnast clinging to her glory days of 12 years earlier. It opened the 2015 Sundance Film Festival.<ref name=5reasons>{{Cite web url=https://variety.com/2015/film/festivals/5-reasons-the-bronze-could-be-a-breakout-comedy-in-2015-1201412800/ title=5 Reasons The Bronze Could be a Breakout Comedy in 2015 last=Setoodeh first=Ramin work=Variety (magazine) Variety  date= January 23, 2015 access-date=April 24, 2016  archivedate= November 6, 2016  archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20161106155528/http://variety.com/2015/film/festivals/5-reasons-the-bronze-could-be-a-breakout-comedy-in-2015-1201412800/  deadurl=no}}</ref> Variety (magazine) Variety said, "Rauch, who co-wrote the screenplay with her husband Winston, has never carried a film before. But she delivers the best breakthrough comedic performance by an actress since Melissa McCarthy in Bridesmaids (2011 film) Bridesmaids."<ref name=5reasons /> Sony Pictures Classics acquired the film in September 2015.<ref>{{Cite web url=http://deadline.com/2015/09/the-bronze-sony-pictures-classics-release-date-relativity-media-1201522219/ title=Ex-Relativity Pic The Bronze Lands at Sony Pictures Classics last=Pedersen first=Erik website=Deadline Hollywood  date= September 11, 2015 access-date=April 24, 2016  archivedate=August 22, 2016   archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20160822214338/http://deadline.com/2015/09/the-bronze-sony-pictures-classics-release-date-relativity-media-1201522219/  deadurl=no}}</ref>

In 2017, Rauch voiced Harley Quinn in the animated film Batman and Harley Quinn and she voiced Light Thief in Light Riders from Blaze and the Monster Machines.

==Personal life==
Rauch is married to writer Winston Rauch (né Beigel), who collaborated with her on The Miss Education of Jenna Bush and other projects.<ref name=nypost /><ref name=Silverman>{{cite journal  first=Stephen M.  last=Silverman  work=People (magazine) People  url=http://www.people.com/people/article/0,,1096008,00.html  title=Jenna Bush Takes Center Stage in New York  date=August 19, 2005  accessdate=February 4, 2015}}</ref> Rauch announced that she was pregnant on July 11, 2017, and disclosed that she had a miscarriage previously.<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.glamour.com/story/actress-melissa-rauch-announces-pregnancy-and-reflects-on-miscarriage title=Big Bang Theory Star Melissa Rauch Announces Her Pregnancy and Reflects on Previous Miscarriage first=Melissa last=Rauch date= website=glamour.com accessdate=September 27, 2017}}</ref> On December 4, 2017 Rauch announced on Instagram that she had given birth to a girl, Sadie.<ref>{{cite journal url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/melissa-rauch-welcomes-a-beautiful-baby-girl-named-sadie_us_5a267e75e4b0f9f0203f0c24 title=Actress Melissa Rauch Welcomes A ‘Beautiful Baby Girl’ Named Sadie work=The Huffington Post}}</ref>

Rauch modeled the voice of List of The Big Bang Theory characters#Bernadette Bernadette after her parents: the "sweet, high-pitched purr" is modeled after her mother, while the "eerie, haranguing imitation" of Mrs. Wolowitz is modeled after her father.<ref name=nypost>{{cite web url=https://nypost.com/2013/03/24/screaming-success/ title=Screaming success last=Getlen first=Larry date=March 24, 2013 work=New York Post accessdate=September 27, 2015}}</ref> Both are "very different" from Rauchs real voice.<ref name=Maxim>{{cite web url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/11/16/melissa-rauch-maxim_n_4288568.html title=Melissa Rauch of Big Bang Theory Does S&M-Inspired Photo Shoot for Maxim date=November 16, 2013 work=The Huffington Post accessdate=September 27, 2015}}</ref>
