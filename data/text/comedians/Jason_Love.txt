
Jason Love is an American comedian, musician, and author from Thousand Oaks, California. Love began his humor career in 2003 by syndicating a daily cartoon, Snapshots, in collaboration with Serbian illustrator Vladimir Stankovski. Snapshots continues to be published by newspapers such as The Denver Post and Tampa Bay Times.

From 2003 to 2007, Love worked as a humor columnist for the Ventura County Star.<ref>{{cite web url=https://s3.amazonaws.com/gigsalad_media/j/jason_love_thousand_oaks/51fcb4a85926a.jpg title=  Former Star columnist Jason Love fills us in on his life as a touring comic » Ventura County Star<!-- Bot generated title --> website=S3.amazonaws.com accessdate=8 May 2018}}</ref>

From 2008 to present, Jason Love has worked full-time as a standup comedian, performing more than 200 times per year. He works routinely for cruise ships such as Royal Caribbean, Holland America, Norwegian, and Carnival.<ref name="dcptalent.com">https://www.dcptalent.com/act/jasonlove/</ref>

Jason does corporate events for companies such as Chevron, Farmers Insurance, and True Religion.<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.GigMasters.com/comedian/jasonlove/feedback title=Jason Love - Reviews website=Gigmasters.com accessdate=8 May 2018}}</ref>

Jason runs Love & Laughter, a non-profit organization that brings free standup comedy shows to the cancer support community.<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.patreon.com/LoveLaughter title=Jason Love is creating Love & Laughter, Live Comedy for Cancer Support Community - Patreon website=Patreon.com accessdate=8 May 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=https://www.tucsonweekly.com/TheRange/archives/2018/04/18/laughing-stock-comedy-for-charity-brings-the-love title=Laughing Stock: Comedy for Charity Brings the Love first=Linda last=Ray website=Tucsonweekly.com accessdate=8 May 2018}}</ref>

Jason appeared on the comedy special “Standup and Deliver” on Nuvo TV.<ref name="thecomicscomic.com">{{cite web url=http://thecomicscomic.com/2014/01/28/nuvotvs-stand-up-and-deliver-goes-to-cabo-san-lucas/ title=NUVOtv’s Stand Up and Deliver goes to Cabo San Lucas date=28 January 2014 website=Thecomicscomic.com accessdate=8 May 2018}}</ref>

In 2017 he recorded for VidAngel a comedy special called Jokes On Me.<ref name="auto">{{cite web url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1g1BBgFM1bA&t=19s title=Jokes On Me first= last=Jason Love date=19 July 2017 accessdate=8 May 2018 publisher=YouTube}}</ref>
