
Chris Distefano (born August 26, 1984) is an American comedian. He stars in MTV and MTV2s TV shows MTV2s Guy Code Guy Code and Girl Code. He also co-hosted Ultimate Beastmaster and the MSG (TV channel) MSG TV show The Bracket.

==Personal life==
Distefano earned a bachelors degree in psychology from St. Josephs College (New York) St. Josephs College and a doctorate in physical therapy from New York Institute of Technology.<ref>http://www.nyit.edu/images/uploads/2013/alumni/NYIT_Vol12No2_Digital_5-19.pdf{{dead link date=November 2016  bot=InternetArchiveBot  fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

He previously dated comedian Carly Aquilino. The couple shared videos documenting moving in together on social media.<ref>http://www.mtv.com/news/2385964/girl-code-carly-aquilino-chris-distefano-sleeping/</ref>

==Career==
Distefano hosted the 2010 Fencing Masters on the SNY network & Elite 8 of the 2011 and 2012 March Madness Comedy Competition at the comedy club Carolines on Broadway. He was selected to headline Carolines Breakout Artist Series and was selected as a finalist in the 2011 NY Comedy Festival. Distefano is a frequent guest on the Opie with Jim Norton show. Distefano headlined his first national U.S. tour in September 2015 with Monster Energy Outbreak Presents.

He is currently one of two American announcers on Netflixs Ultimate Beastmaster for Season 2 that released on the streaming service December 15, 2017.
