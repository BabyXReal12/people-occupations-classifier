Dennis Michael Miller (born November 3, 1953) is an American comedian, talk show host, political commentator, sports commentator and actor.

He was a cast member of Saturday Night Live from 1985 to 1991, and subsequently hosted a string of his own talk shows on HBO, CNBC, and in syndication. From 2007 to 2015, Miller hosted a daily, three-hour, self-titled talk radio program, nationally syndicated by Westwood One (current) Westwood One.
