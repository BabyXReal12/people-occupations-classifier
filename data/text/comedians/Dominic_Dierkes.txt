
Dominic Dierkes (born March 19, 1984) is an United States American actor, comedian and writer best known for his involvement in the sketch comedy group Derrick Comedy.

==Early life==
Dierkes was born and raised in Memphis, Tennessee.<ref name="BA">{{cite web title=BA #076: Dominic Dierkes url=http://boxangeles.com/2015/04/27/ba-076-dominic-dierkes website=the Box Angeles podcast}}</ref> He began doing stand-up comedy at the age of 14 and since he was underage his parents had to be present at his sets.<ref name="BA"/>

Dierkes attended the Tisch School of the Arts where he majored in film/television production.<ref name="BA"/> There he joined the sketch comedy group Hammerkatz NYU alongside future Derrick Comedy members Donald Glover and DC Pierson.<ref name="BA"/> During this time Dierkes began taking classes at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre and both of his comedy groups performed there regularly.<ref name="BA"/>

==Career==
With Derrick Comedy gaining notoriety through their comedic videos on YouTube, the group subsequently wrote and starred in the 2009 comedy Mystery Team.<ref>[https://movies.nytimes.com/2009/12/04/movies/04mystery.html College Humor on the Big Screen], nytimes.com, December 4, 2009.</ref> Dierkes is a former writer for ONN, the "television" branch of satirical newspaper The Onion.{{citation needed date=February 2016}}

Dierkes has appeared in such television shows as Parks and Recreation, Community (TV series) Community, The Nighttime Clap, and Mad Men. He had a recurring role on the Showtime (TV channel) Showtime show Weeds (TV series) Weeds.<ref  name="imdb"/> He and his writing partner, Sean Clements,<ref name="BA"/> were writers on the short-lived Fox Broadcasting Company Fox animated series Allen Gregory in 2011. In 2012, they joined the writing staff of Workaholics for its third season and guest starred in the 15th episode, "Webcam Girl" as Keith the owner of the site.<ref name="imdb">{{IMDb name 2257388}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Dierkes is a teetotaller since moving to Los Angeles.<ref name="BA"/>
