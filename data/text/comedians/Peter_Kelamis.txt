Peter Kelamis (born December 11, 1967) is an Australian-born Canadian actor, voice actor and comedian.
He is arguably best known for playing Dr. Adam Brody in Stargate Universe, Goku in Oceans English dubs of the animated series Dragon Ball Z, and Rolf from Ed, Edd n Eddy.

==Career==
Some of his appearances on TV were in Eureka (U.S. TV series) Eureka, The Outer Limits (1995 TV series) The Outer Limits, Corner Gas, Sliders, Stargate SG-1, Stargate Universe, The Sentinel (TV series) The Sentinel, NCIS (TV series) NCIS and The X-Files. Film appearances include; Happy Gilmore, Fear of Flying, Ill Be Home for Christmas, Best in Show (film) Best in Show, The Sport Pages: The Heidi Bowl, and Everythings Gone Green (film) Everythings Gone Green. He also had a recurring role in Beggars and Choosers (TV series) Beggars and Choosers.

As a voice actor, he replaced Ian James Corlett as the voice of Goku in Funimation Entertainment FUNimation/Saban Entertainment Sabans short-lived Ocean Productions original dub of Dragon Ball Dragon Ball Z, and also voiced Goku in FUNimation and Geneon Universal Entertainment Pioneers dub of the Dragon Ball Z movies Dragon Ball Z: Dead Zone Dead Zone, Dragon Ball Z: The Worlds Strongest The Worlds Strongest, and Dragon Ball Z: The Tree of Might The Tree of Might. During 2000, Kelamis reprised the role in an unrelated dub produced by Ocean Productions Westwood Media and AB Groupe for the UK. He left in the middle of the Cell (Dragon Ball Z) Cell arc to pursue other projects, with Kirby Morrow voicing Goku for the remainder of the production. He has also famously voiced Rolf in Ed, Edd n Eddy as well as Whiplash (comics) Whiplash in Iron Man: Armored Adventures. Kelamis originally auditioned to play the Eds, as well as the other male characters in Ed, Edd n Eddy but didnt land either role. Instead he was confronted by Danny Antonucci to play the character of Rolf. He also voiced Reaper in the video game Devil Kings as well as providing the voice of Byrne in the CGI animation, Dreamkix.
