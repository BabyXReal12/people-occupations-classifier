Judith Therese Evans (born July 20, 1975),<ref name="Greer 2014">{{cite book  last=Greer  first=Judy  year=2014  title=I Dont Know What You Know Me From: Confessions of a Co-Star  url=  location=New York  publisher=Doubleday (publisher) Doubleday  page=  isbn=978-0-385-53788-9 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=http://www.ctvnews.ca/entertainment/judy-greer-laments-rom-com-decline-while-promoting-new-book-1.1768644  title=Judy Greer laments rom-com decline while promoting new book  publisher=CTV News  date=April 9, 2014  accessdate=April 21, 2014  first=Victoria  last=Ahearn }}</ref><ref name=allmovie>{{cite web  url = https://www.nytimes.com/movies/person/235491/Judy-Greer/biography   title= Judy Greer; Alternate Name: Judy Evans Greer, Judith Laura Evans, Judy Evans, Judith Laura Evans  publisher= AllMovie / Rovi via The New York Times   first=Andrea   last=LeVasseur accessdate= September 26, 2014}}</ref> known as Judy Greer, is an American actress, model, and author. Her notable television roles include List of recurring Arrested Development characters#Kitty Sanchez Kitty Sanchez in the comedy series Arrested Development (TV series) Arrested Development, Ingrid Nelson in the comedy series Its Always Sunny In Philadelphia, Trixie in the comedy-drama series Californication (TV series) Californication, and Cheryl Tunt in the animated comedy series Archer (TV series) Archer.

In film, Greer is known for her supporting roles in romantic comedy romantic comedies, with appearances in What Women Want (2000), The Wedding Planner (2001), 13 Going on 30 (2004), 27 Dresses (2008) and Love & Other Drugs (2010). Her other notable film appearances include roles in Adaptation (film) Adaptation (2002), The Village (2004 film) The Village (2004), The Descendants (2011), Carrie (2013 film) Carrie (2013), Jurassic World (2015), and Halloween (2018 film) Halloween (2018). Greer has also portrayed Cornelia in the Planet of the Apes Planet of the Apes#Reboot film series reboot series. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, she portrays Maggie Lang in Ant-Man (film) Ant-Man (2015) and Ant-Man and the Wasp (2018).

In 2011, Greer began hosting an online series of workout videos called Reluctantly Healthy, which was adapted into a series by Litton Entertainment in 2014 as part of their Saturday morning One Magnificent Morning block for The CW.<ref name=Rel>{{cite web url=http://screen.yahoo.com/women/reluctantly-healthy/  archive-url=https://archive.is/20130209190319/http://screen.yahoo.com/women/reluctantly-healthy/  dead-url=yes  archive-date=2013-02-09  title=Reluctantly Healthy Video  publisher=Yahoo!  accessdate=June 16, 2017  df= }}</ref> Also in 2014, she released her first autobiographical novel, titled I Dont Know What You Know Me From: Confessions of a Co-Star.<ref>{{cite news last1=Luberecki first1=Beth title=Judy Greer dishes on her new book I Dont Know What You Know Me From url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/express/wp/2014/05/14/judy-greer-dishes-on-her-new-book-i-dont-know-what-you-know-me-from/ accessdate=July 14, 2014 publisher=The Washingtonpost date=May 14, 2014}}</ref>

==Early life==
She was born Judith Therese Evans in Detroit, Michigan. Her mother, Mollie Ann (née Greer), is a hospital administrator, and her father, Rich Evans, is a mechanical engineer.<ref name="Greer 2014"/><ref>{{cite web url=https://www.theguardian.com/film/2012/dec/27/judy-greer-arrested-development-get-flashed  title=Judy Greer: I get flashed all the time. Its mostly men  publisher=The Guardian  date=December 27, 2012  first=Hermione  last=Hoby }}</ref> Her mother was once a nun who left the convent after eight years, when she was "kicked out" for wild behavior, including owning a red bathing suit.<ref name="colbert2017">{{cite news title=Judy Greer Starred Alongside Stephen In A Rejected 2002 Pilot url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NZ05TIoEYUM accessdate=June 16, 2017 work=The Late Show with Stephen Colbert date=June 16, 2017}}</ref> Greer was raised Catholic and grew up in Redford Charter Township, Michigan Redford Township and Livonia, Michigan Livonia.<ref>{{cite web   url = http://www.dvdtalk.com/interviews/judy_greer_13_g.html  title = Judy Greer - 13 Going on 30  accessdate = October 12, 2011  last = Henbest  first = Danielle  date =   work = DVD Talk}}</ref> She attended Winston Churchill High School (Wayne County, Michigan) Churchill High School<ref>{{cite web   url = http://detnews.com/article/20100701/ENT10/7010315/1457/ENT10   title = Judy Greer is eager to work in Detroit   accessdate = July 30, 2010   last = Toby   first = Mekeisha   date = July 1, 2010   work = The Detroit News}}{{dead link  date=September 2014}}</ref> and graduated from The Theatre School at DePaul University in 1997.<ref>{{cite web   url = http://theatreschool.depaul.edu/special_programs_awards.php   title = Awards For Excellence In The Arts   accessdate = July 30, 2010   publisher = The Theater School at DePaul University   archivedate= August 13, 2006   archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20060813042958/http://theatreschool.depaul.edu/special_programs_awards.php}}</ref> She later adopted her mothers maiden name for her stage name, as there were several other actresses named Judy or Judi Evans.

==Personal life==
Greer is married to Dean E. Johnsen, an executive producer of Real Time with Bill Maher.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/actress-judy-greer-marries-dean-johnson-20111912 title=Actress Judy Greer Marries Dean Johnsen author=Zach Johnson date=December 19, 2011 publisher=usmagazine.com accessdate=2012-02-01}}</ref>
