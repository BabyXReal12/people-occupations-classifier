ONeal became known for his conversational and often confrontational point of view stand-up. His profile rose through appearances on Tough Crowd with Colin Quinn and, from 2002,  Opie and Anthony. He hosted The Black Phillip Show on radio from 2006 to 2008. ONeal died of a stroke resulting from his Diabetes mellitus type 2  diabetes on November 29, 2011, at the age of 41.

==Early life==
Patrice Lumumba Malcolm ONeal was born on December 7, 1969 in New York City.
