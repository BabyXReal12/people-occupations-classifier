William Alan "Will" Friedle (born August 11, 1976) is an United States American actor, voice actor, and writer.
He is best known for his comedic roles, most notably the underachieving elder brother Eric Matthews (Boy Meets World) Eric Matthews on the long-running TV sitcom Boy Meets World from 1993 to 2000. More recently, he has voiced a number of animated characters such as Terry McGinnis Terry McGinnis/Batman, the title character of Batman Beyond, and Ron Stoppable of Kim Possible. He voices Deadpool in Ultimate Spider-Man (TV series) Ultimate Spider-Man and Star-Lord in the animated version of Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series) Guardians of the Galaxy, replacing Chris Cox. He also performed the voices of Doyle in The Secret Saturdays, Lion-O in the rebooted ThunderCats (2011 TV series) ThunderCats series, and Blue Beetle (Jaime Reyes) Blue Beetle on Batman: The Brave and the Bold.
One of his most recent roles has been the speaking voice of Bumblebee (Transformers) Bumblebee in the final episode of Transformers: Prime and the movie Transformers Prime Beast Hunters: Predacons Rising and in the stand-alone sequel series Transformers: Robots in Disguise (2015 TV series) Transformers: Robots in Disguise, as well as in Transformers: Rescue Bots.

In December 2013, it was announced that Friedle would reprise his Boy Meets World role of Eric Matthews on the continuation series Girl Meets World.
==Early life==
Friedle was born in Hartford, Connecticut, the son of Patricia Joan (née Leary) and Gary Allen Friedle, both lawyers.
After committing himself to becoming an actor, Friedle continually commuted from Avon, Connecticut to New York City for auditions. He graduated from Avon High School (Connecticut) Avon High School in 1994.

Friedle attended Occidental College. In the July 4, 2006 issue of People (magazine) People, Friedle mentioned he had thought about attending culinary school.

==Career==
After co-hosting Nickelodeon (TV channel) Nickelodeons Dont Just Sit There for three seasons (1988–1991), Friedle was cast as Cory Matthews likeable but underachieving elder brother List of Boy Meets World characters Eric on the long-running TV sitcom Boy Meets World from 1993 to 2000.

In 2004, he co-starred with Chris Owen (actor) Chris Owen in the independent film National Lampoons Gold Diggers (originally titled Lady Killers). He also appeared in a supporting role in the 2005 ABC Family movie Everything You Want (film) Everything You Want. Friedle was cast as Mike in the The WB Television Network WB sitcom Off Centre, but could not accept the role because of a previous commitment to the UPN series The Random Years. The role ended up going to Eddie Kaye Thomas.
Friedle voiced Seifer Almasy in the English language English version of the video game Kingdom Hearts II, Gideon Wyeth in the English version of the video game Advent Rising, and Jaster Rogue in the English version of the video game Rogue Galaxy.

==Personal life==
In 1997, Friedle dated United States American actress Jennifer Love Hewitt,
and co-starred with her in the romantic teen comedy Trojan War (film) Trojan War. Hewitt made a guest appearance as Jennifer Love "Feffy" Fefferman on Boy Meets World episode "And Then There Was Shawn".

Friedle is best friends with actor Jason Marsden and was the best man at Marsdens wedding in October 2004. The two have also worked together in numerous productions: Boy Meets World, Trojan War (film) Trojan War, Static Shock, Kim Possible, Batman Beyond, Justice League Unlimited, Batman: The Brave and the Bold, ThunderCats (2011 TV series) ThunderCats and Mad (TV series) Mad.

He married his girlfriend, Susan Martens, on September 25, 2016 in Connecticut.
