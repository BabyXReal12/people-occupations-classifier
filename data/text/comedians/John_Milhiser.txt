
John Milhiser (born November 29, 1981)<ref name="nbc"/> is an Americans American actor and comedian who was a cast member on Saturday Night Live for the Saturday Night Live (season 39) 2013–2014 season.<ref name=new>{{cite web   last =Abramovich   first =Seth   title =Cheat Sheet: Get to Know the 5 New Faces of SNL   work =The Live Feed   publisher =The Hollywood Reporter   date =11 September 2013   url =http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/saturday-night-live-get-know-625783   accessdate = 7 October 2013}}</ref>

==Career==
He has been a regular performer at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater since 2005, where he was a member of the sketch comedy group Serious Lunch, who have been featured on Late Night with Jimmy Fallon and Attack of the Show. Milhiser is a native of Belle Mead, New Jersey and he graduated from Montgomery High School (New Jersey) Montgomery High School in Skillman, New Jersey. He later attended Hofstra University, where he graduated in 2004 as a Film Studies and Production major<ref>{{cite web   last =Walter   first =Geoffrey   title =Hofstra Graduate Cast on New Season of SNL   work =Bellmore Patch: News: Around Bellmore   publisher =Patch   date =17 September 2013   url =http://bellmore.patch.com/groups/around-town/p/hofstra-graduate-cast-on-new-season-of-snl   accessdate = 7 October 2013}}</ref> and was a member of Sigma Pi fraternity.<ref>{{cite web  title =Notable Alumni - Sigma Pi Fraternity publisher =Sigma Pi Fraternity  url =https://sigmapi.org/notable-alumni/}}</ref>

Milhiser made his debut on Saturday Night Live on the September 28, 2013 season premiere hosted by Tina Fey with musical guest Arcade Fire. His celebrity impressions included Jon Cryer, Matthew McConaughey, Verne Troyer (as Mini-Me from the Austin Powers movies), Justin Timberlake, and Billie Joe Armstrong. On July 15, 2014, it was announced that Milhisers contract with SNL was not renewed and he would not be returning as a cast member.<ref name="avclub">{{Cite news  url=http://www.avclub.com/article/noel-wells-and-john-milhiser-are-also-leaving-satu-206958  title=Noël Wells and John Milhiser Are Also Leaving Saturday Night Live  last=ONeal  first=Sean  date=July 15, 2014  work=The A.V. Club accessdate=July 19, 2014}}</ref>

In 2014, Milhiser appeared in a supporting role in the indie film Camp Takota starring comedians Grace Helbig, Hannah Hart and Mamrie Hart. He has also made guest appearances on television programs such as Adam Ruins Everything, 2 Broke Girls, Love (TV series) Netflix Original Series Love, and Other Space.
