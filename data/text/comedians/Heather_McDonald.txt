Heather Ann McDonald (born June 14, 1970) is an American actress, comedian and author. Born and educated in Southern California, she is best known for her appearances on the E! series Chelsea Lately. She was one of the eight writers on the show and often participated in sketches and segments. McDonald also write and appeared in the shows spin-off, After Lately. Her first book, a 2010 memoir of her college years, made the Bestseller List of the New York Times.
