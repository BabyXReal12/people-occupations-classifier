Mark Steel (born 4 July 1960) is an English comedian, broadcaster, newspaper columnist and author.
A Stand-up comedy stand-up comedian known for his left-wing beliefs (he was a long-standing member of the Socialist Workers Party (UK) Socialist Workers Party)
he has made many appearances on radio and television shows as a guest panellist, and has written regular columns in The Guardian and The Independent. He is perhaps best known for presenting The Mark Steel Lectures, The Mark Steel Revolution, The Mark Steel Solution and Mark Steels in Town.

==Early life==
Steel was adopted 10 days after he was born.
His adoptive father worked in insurance and his mother was a housewife who supplemented the familys income through factory work and working as a lollipop lady.
He had a close relationship with his adoptive parents.
