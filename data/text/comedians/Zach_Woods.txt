
Zach Woods (born September 25, 1984) is an American actor and comedian. He is best known for starring as Jared Dunn on the HBO comedy series Silicon Valley (TV series) Silicon Valley. Prior to that, he was a series regular for 3 seasons on the NBC sitcom The Office (U.S. TV series) The Office, playing the role of Gabe Lewis. He also recurs on the HBO series Veep (TV series) Veep and on the USA Network sitcom Playing House (TV series) Playing House.

==Early life==
Woods was born in Trenton, New Jersey.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://www.lamag.com/culturefiles/meet-zach-woods-the-anxious-heart-of-silicon-valley/ title=Meet Zach Woods, the Anxious Heart of "Silicon Valley" - Los Angeles Magazine date=2014-05-29 work=Los Angeles Magazine access-date=2017-02-26 language=en-US}}</ref> He is Jewish.<ref name="podcast">{{cite web url=http://www.wtfpod.com/podcast/episodes/episode_597_-_zach_woods  title=Episode 597 - Zach Woods}}</ref> Woods father is a trained psychiatrist and social worker who specializes in clinical therapy, and his mother is a nurse practitioner.<ref name="podcast" /> As a child, he participated in both Karate and Little League Baseball. Woods is a middle child; he has an older brother and a younger sister.<ref name="podcast" /> During 1997 and 1998, he also attended Interlochen Center for the Arts for summer camp.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://crescendo.interlochen.org/story/and-nominee-interlochen-grammys-golden-globes-and-academy-awards title=And the nominee is ... Interlochen at the Grammys, Golden Globes and Academy Awards}}</ref> Woods grew up in Yardley, Pennsylvania, and graduated from Pennsbury High School in 2003. He is a graduate of New York University.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://www.interviewmagazine.com/culture/zach-woods/ title=Zach Woods and Human Comedy work=Interview Magazine access-date=2017-02-26}}</ref>

==Career==
File:Bobby Moynihan & Zach Woods, Del Close Marathon 2.09.jpg thumb Woods with Bobby Moynihan in 2015
He started performing improv at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre at age 16 and was a part of the Improvisational comedy improvisational sketch troupe "The Stepfathers", whose members also include Bobby Moynihan and Chris Gethard.<ref>{{cite web url=https://ucbtheatre.com/show/717  title=The Stepfathers  publisher=Newyork.ucbtheatre.com  date=  accessdate=2013-05-20}}</ref> He has taught improv at Columbia University, Duke University and Lincoln Center.<ref>{{cite web title=UCB Theatre – Zach Woods url=http://ucbcomedy.com/user/314}}</ref> He has appeared in such films as In the Loop, The Other Guys and appeared in the CollegeHumor skit "Adam and Eve in the Friends Zone".

Woods is also known for starring as Awkward Boy, the protagonist in the YouTube video series The Most Awkward Boy in the World.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.ucbcomedy.com/talent/view/38/zach-woods  title=Zach Woods • UCB Comedy  publisher=Ucbcomedy.com  date=  accessdate=2013-05-20  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130708122646/http://www.ucbcomedy.com/talent/view/38/zach-woods  archivedate=July 8, 2013  df=mdy-all }}</ref> In 2010, Woods appeared as a zombie in a Starburst (confectionery) Starburst candy commercial.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://adweek.blogs.com/adfreak/30-freakiest-ads-of-2010.html  title=Starburst "Zombie" Commercial  author=Tim Nudd  date=December 1, 2010  work=The 30 Freakiest Ads of 2010  publisher=AdFreak.com  accessdate=February 6, 2011}}</ref>

Starting in 2010, he portrayed Gabe Lewis on the National Broadcasting Company NBC sitcom The Office (U.S. TV series) The Office. He was promoted to series regular beginning in The Office (U.S. season 7) Season 7.<ref>{{cite web url=http://ausiellofiles.ew.com/2010/06/16/office-promotes-zach-woods/ archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100618023611/http://ausiellofiles.ew.com/2010/06/16/office-promotes-zach-woods/ dead-url=yes archive-date=June 18, 2010 title=Exclusive: The Office promotes Gabe to series regular work=Entertainment Weeklys EW.com date=June 16, 2010 accessdate=June 16, 2010}}</ref>

Since 2014, Woods has costarred as Jared on HBOs Silicon Valley (TV series) Silicon Valley. He also appeared on HBOs Veep (TV series) Veep as Ed Webster.

Woods had a role in the action comedy Spy (2015 film) Spy (2015), alongside Melissa McCarthy, Jason Statham, Rose Byrne, Jude Law, Allison Janney, and 50 Cent.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://deadline.com/2014/05/adrian-lester-joins-grey-lady-zach-woods-cast-in-spy-723488/ title=Adrian Lester Joins ‘Grey Lady’; Zach Woods Cast In ‘Spy’ date=2014-05-02 work=Deadline access-date=2017-02-26 language=en-US}}</ref> He appeared in Feigs follow-up movie Ghostbusters (2016 film) Ghostbusters in 2016.
