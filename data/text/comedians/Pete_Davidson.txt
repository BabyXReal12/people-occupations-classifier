Pete Davidson (born November 16, 1993)
is an American comedian and actor. He is a cast member on Saturday Night Live. Davidson has also appeared on the MTV shows Guy Code, Wild n Out, and Failosophy.
He has performed stand-up comedy on Adam Devines House Party, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, and Comedy Underground with Dave Attell, and guest-starred in Brooklyn Nine-Nine.
