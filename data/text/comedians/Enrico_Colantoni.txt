
Enrico Colantoni (born February 14, 1963) is a Canadian actor and Director, best known for portraying Elliot DiMauro in the sitcom Just Shoot Me!, Keith Mars on the television series Veronica Mars, and Sergeant Greg Parker on the television series Flashpoint (TV Series) Flashpoint. He has also had supporting roles in such films as The Wrong Guy,  Galaxy Quest, A.I. Artificial Intelligence, and Contagion (film) Contagion, and guest appearances on Monk (TV series) Monk, Numbers (TV series) Numb3rs, Party Down, Stargate SG-1, and Bones (TV series) Bones. More recently, he starred as Allen Conner in Remedy (TV series) Remedy, and played crime boss Carl Elias in a recurring role on Person of Interest (TV series) Person of Interest. He played Laura Hollis father in season three of the online web series Carmilla (series) Carmilla. He most recently appeared as Vincent Ingram in Travelers (TV series) Travelers.

Colantoni Directed two episodes of the TV show iZombie (TV series) iZombie.

==Early life==
Colantoni was born in Toronto, Ontario, the son of Gina, a garment worker, and Quintino Colantoni, a labourer and truck driver.<ref>[http://www.filmreference.com/film/26/Enrico-Colantoni.html "Enrico Colantoni Biography (1963-)"]. Film Reference. Retrieved April 18, 2015.</ref> Both his parents were immigrants from Italy, and his brother, Det. Sgt. Hector Colantoni, is a retired police officer with the Toronto Police Service.<ref name=Eggplante>{{cite news author=Kalanderopoulos, Christopher title=Asking for Help url=http://www.eggplante.com/2011/02/23/asking-for-help/ newspaper=Eggplante! date=February 23, 2011}}</ref>

He attended the University of Toronto studying psychology and sociology, but transferred to the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City.  Colantoni graduated from the Yale School of Drama, winning the Carol Dye Award.<ref>{{cite news  url=https://movies.nytimes.com/person/271316/Enrico-Colantoni/biography  work=The New York Times title=Enrico Colantoni accessdate=April 18, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news  url=https://www.thestar.com/entertainment/2008/07/10/flashpoint_star_brings_work_home_to_toronto.html  title=Flashpoint star brings work home to Toronto work=Toronto Star first1=Bruce last1=DeMara date=10 July 2008}}</ref>

==Philanthropy==
Since 2011, Colantoni has been heavily involved with The Tema Conter Memorial Trust, an organization that assists first responders and other service personnel deal with post-traumatic stress disorder. He became their official spokesperson and has been involved in their annual tribute gala in Toronto every year since.<ref name=Eggplante/>

He has also been involved in the Companion Animal Protection Societys campaign to boycott commercial pet stores, creating a video to raise awareness about the topic.<ref>[http://www.caps-web.org/news-and-headlines/item/499-caps-psa-with-enrico-colantoni "CAPS PSA with Enrico Colantoni "]. Companion Animal Protection Society. December 21, 2011.</ref>
