
Todd Joseph Miller (born June 4, 1981) is an American actor, stand-up comedian, producer, and writer.<ref name=NYTimes-QA-SiliconValley-2016>{{cite news last1=Ryzik first1=Melena title=Q. and A. With T.J. Miller: ‘Silicon Valley’ and the Mucinex Phlegm Ball url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/04/24/arts/television/q-and-a-with-tj-miller-silicon-valley-and-the-mucinex-phlegm-ball.html work=The New York Times date=April 20, 2016}}</ref>

In 2008, he made his acting debut in Cloverfield, and from 2010 to 2019, he voiced Tuffnut Thornton in all three How to Train Your Dragon (franchise) How to Train Your Dragon films. From 2014 to 2017, he starred as Erlich Bachman in the HBO sitcom Silicon Valley (TV series) Silicon Valley,<ref name=NewYorker-SiliconValley-2016>{{cite news last1=Marantz first1=Andrew title=How Silicon Valley Nails Silicon Valley url=https://www.newyorker.com/culture/culture-desk/how-silicon-valley-nails-silicon-valley work=The New Yorker date=June 9, 2016}}</ref> and also played Marvel Comics character Weasel (Marvel Comics) Weasel in 2016s Deadpool (film) Deadpool and its  2018 sequel, Deadpool 2. Miller also has had starring roles in films such as Yogi Bear (film) Yogi Bear, Shes Out of My League, Transformers: Age of Extinction, Big Hero 6 (film) Big Hero 6, Office Christmas Party, The Emoji Movie and Ready Player One (film) Ready Player One.<ref name=DenverPost-HighPlainsComedyFest-2017>{{cite news last1=Wenzel first1=John title=T.J.
