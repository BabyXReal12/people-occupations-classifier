Todd Joseph Miller (born June 4, 1981) is an American actor, stand-up comedian, producer, and writer.
In 2008, he made his acting debut in Cloverfield, and from 2010 to 2019, he voiced Tuffnut Thornton in all three How to Train Your Dragon (franchise) How to Train Your Dragon films. From 2014 to 2017, he starred as Erlich Bachman in the HBO sitcom Silicon Valley (TV series) Silicon Valley,
and also played Marvel Comics character Weasel (Marvel Comics) Weasel in 2016s Deadpool (film) Deadpool and its  2018 sequel, Deadpool 2. Miller also has had starring roles in films such as Yogi Bear (film) Yogi Bear, Shes Out of My League, Transformers: Age of Extinction, Big Hero 6 (film) Big Hero 6, Office Christmas Party, The Emoji Movie and Ready Player One (film) Ready Player One.
==Early life and education==
Miller was born in Denver Denver, Colorado, the son of Leslie Miller, a clinical psychologist, and Kent Miller, an attorney from Chanute, Kansas.
His Christian father is of English, Swedish, German, and Scottish ancestry, whereas his Jewish mother is of German, Austrian, and Russian descent.
He attended Graland Graland Country Day School and graduated from Denvers East High School (Denver) East High School, where he participated in drama productions.
