Vanessa Bayer (born {{start date 1981 11 14}})<ref name="nbc"/> is an American actress and comedian, best known as a cast member on Saturday Night Live, for which she earned a Primetime Emmy nomination as well as for supporting roles in the  films Trainwreck (film) Trainwreck (2015), Office Christmas Party (2016), Carrie Pilby (film) Carrie Pilby (2016), and Ibiza (film) Ibiza (2018).

==Early life==
Bayer was born and raised in Orange, Ohio, a suburb of Cleveland, to a Reform Jewish family.<ref name=FabFour>Moynihan, Rob. "SNLs Fab Four", TV Guide, March 7, 2011, Pages 44-45</ref><!-- http://www.cleveland.com/dining/index.ssf/2012/11/saturday_night_lives_vanessa_b.html says she was in Moreland Hills, Ohio --> She is the daughter of Carolyn (née Polster) and Todd Bayer.<ref name="orangeschools">[http://www.orangeschools.org/news/view/334 Vanessa Bayer] from the Orange High School (Ohio) Orange High School website</ref> She has a brother, Jonah, who is a music journalist and the drummer for the punk supergroup United Nations (band) United Nations.<ref>{{cite web last1=Eakin first1=Marah title=Jonah and Vanessa Bayer on Sound Advice, sibling rivalries, and childhood bribery url=https://www.avclub.com/jonah-and-vanessa-bayer-on-sound-advice-sibling-rivalr-1798282072 website=The A.V. Club accessdate=20 December 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web title=Watch Vanessa Bayer Roast United Nations url=https://www.stereogum.com/1697183/watch-vanessa-bayer-roast-united-nations/video/ website=Stereogum accessdate=20 December 2017 date=6 August 2014}}</ref>

At the age of 15, she was diagnosed with leukemia. Bayer stated that while battling the disease, she discovered the meaning of “comic relief”. “I don’t know if it made me funnier, but it was so amazing, how it made everything be O.K.,” she said.<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.nytimes.com/2016/05/08/nyregion/the-very-lazy-sunday-routine-of-vanessa-bayer-of-saturday-night-live.html?_r=0 title=The (Very Lazy) Sunday Routine of Vanessa Bayer of ‘Saturday Night Live’ last=Correal first=Annie date=May 7, 2016 website=The New York Times publisher=The New York Times Company access-date=October 30, 2016}}</ref>

Bayer graduated from Orange High School (Ohio) Orange High School in 2000<ref>{{cite web author=  url=http://www.cleveland.com/dining/index.ssf/2012/11/saturday_night_lives_vanessa_b.html  title=Saturday Night Live regular Vanessa Bayer, a Moreland Hills native, returns home to perform Sunday at the Improv  publisher=cleveland.com  date=  accessdate=2013-10-03}}</ref> and attended the University of Pennsylvania, where she majored in communication and French,<ref>{{cite web author=  url=http://www.clevelandjewishnews.com/features/leisure/entertainment/article_4548880c-3335-11e2-bf5a-0019bb2963f4.html  title=For Saturday Night Live’s Bayer, humor a family affair - Cleveland Jewish News: Entertainment  publisher=Cleveland Jewish News  date=2012-11-20  accessdate=2013-10-03}}</ref> graduating in 2004.

==Career==

As of 2012, Bayer was collaborating with her brother Jonah on the web series Sound Advice for Above Average Productions Above Average. Bayer stars as Janessa Slater, a media coach who helps famous musicians change their image.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.upenn.edu/gazette/0511/pro03.html  title=Penn Gazette &#124; Alumni : Profiles  publisher=Upenn.edu  date=  accessdate=2012-09-22}}</ref><ref>{{cite web author= url=http://jonahbayer.wordpress.com  title=Jonah Bayer  publisher=Jonahbayer.wordpress.com  date=2010-09-21  accessdate=2012-09-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/vb7953/octobers-very-owned-ep-16-an-interview-with-a-guy-who-hung-out-with-drake-for-two-hours-once title=An Interview with a Guy Who Hung Out with Drake for Two Hours Once date=2017-10-16 work=Noisey access-date=2017-10-17 language=en-us}}</ref>

While attending college, Bayer interned on the television shows Sesame Street and Late Night with Conan OBrien and participated in the cast of Bloomers, an all-female musical and sketch comedy troupe.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.bloomerscomedy.com  title=All-Female Musical and Sketch Comedy at the University of Pennsylvania  publisher=Bloomers Comedy  date=  accessdate=2012-09-22}}</ref> Bayer was part of the all-Jewish cast of The Second City Second Citys stage show Jewsical: The Musical, a musical that presented a comedic take on Jewish life and culture.<ref>{{cite web url=http://letmypeoplegrow.org/2011/02/jews-newsrachel-specter-grammys-snl/  title=Jews in the News: Rachel Specter, Grammys & Barbra Streisand publisher=Letmypeoplegrow.org accessdate=2012-09-22}}</ref>

Bayer played the role of Kate Clark in the Chicago-based feature comedy Off the Cuff.{{cn date=December 2017}}

Bayer performed improvisational comedy at Chicagos ImprovOlympic, where she trained with SNL castmate Paul Brittain<ref name=FabFour/> as well as the Annoyance Theatre and The Second City Second City.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.nbcuniversal.presscentre.com/content/detail.aspx?ReleaseID=1683&NewsAreaId=2 title=NBCS SATURDAY NIGHT LIVE PREMIERES SEPTEMBER 25 WITH SNL ALUM AMY POEHLER & CHART-TOPPING MUSICAL GUEST KATY PERRY publisher=NBC-Universal Press Release date=September 17, 2010 accessdate=2012-09-22 archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120311215526/http://www.nbcuniversal.presscentre.com/content/detail.aspx?ReleaseID=1683&NewsAreaId=2 archive-date=March 11, 2012 dead-url=yes df=mdy-all}}</ref>

===Saturday Night Live===
Bayer joined the cast of Saturday Night Live as a featured player on September 25, 2010. Beginning with the 2012–2013 season, Bayer was promoted to repertory status.  She has been praised for her performance on the show, with HitFix calling her "one of the strongest members of the ensemble"{{Verify quote date=July 2017}} and Rolling Stone calling her "Saturday Night Lives secret weapon".{{Verify quote date=July 2017}}  In 2014, she was nominated for an American Comedy Award for her work on the show.{{cn date=December 2017}} Bayer departed SNL on May 20, 2017 after seven years on the show. She held the record of the shows longest-serving female cast member, with 149 episodes (Maya Rudolph, who had been a cast member for 9 seasons, stayed for 146 episodes).<ref>{{cite web url=https://news.avclub.com/vanessa-bayer-is-leaving-snl-1798262112 title=Vanessa Bayer is leaving SNL first=William last=Hughes publisher=}}</ref>
<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.cnn.com/2017/05/20/entertainment/vanessa-bayer-leaving-snl/ title=Vanessa Bayer leaving SNL publisher=CNN date=May 21, 2017 accessdate=2017-05-22}}</ref> She was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series for that season.<ref>{{cite web   url = http://www.emmys.com/awards/nominees-winners/2017/outstanding-supporting-actress-in-a-comedy-series   title = Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series - 2017   publisher = Academy of Television Arts & Sciences   location = Los Angeles   accessdate = 2017-07-13 }}</ref>

Her recurring characters include:
* Laura Parsons, a child actress always performing roles originally played by adults, including from Forrest Gump, The Wolf of Wall Street (2013 film) The Wolf of Wall Street, Brokeback Mountain, and Dallas Buyers Club
* Brecky, a former porn star who creates homemade commercials to obtain free luxury items alongside Cecily Strong
* Rebecca Stern-Markowicz, a co-host of J-Pop America Fun Time Now which celebrates Japanese culture alongside Taran Killam and Jason Sudeikis
* Miss Meadows, an unrealistically perky poetry teacher trying to connect with her delinquent students
* An unnamed director of talk show Mornin Miami
* Jacob the Bar Mitzvah Boy, who appears on Weekend Update to perform a rehearsed speech (complete with forced jokes) about a given subject
* Rachel Green from the sitcom Friends (and other characters from the show)
* One of two workshop elves who perform their jobs poorly in the hope that their master will "punish" them, alongside Kenan Thompson
* An unnamed housewife who makes Totinos Totinos for her "hungry guys".
* Dawn Lazarus, an inept meteorologist who appears on Weekend Update and struggles to discuss the weather.
* Maggie Margaret Bond, whose "walking music" may be unflattering, in SNLs [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Quqfky-zpvA "Soap Opera Reunion"].

==Philanthropy==
Bayer suffered from leukemia as a teenager. The Make-A-Wish Foundation granted her wish to send her family on vacation to Hawaii. As a former "Make-A-Wish kid", Bayer continues to do work with the foundation. In 2015, she hosted the foundations "Evening of Wishes Dinner" to help raise funds for wishes for children with life-threatening illnesses.<ref name="orangeschools"/><ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.ew.com/article/2015/06/22/vanessa-bayer-reveals-childhood-struggle-leukemia publisher=Entertainment Weekly edition=online title=Vanessa Bayer talks to People about her childhood struggle with leukemia last=Daley first=Megan date=2015-06-22 accessdate=2016-09-19}}</ref>
