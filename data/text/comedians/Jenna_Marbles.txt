Jenna Nicole Mourey (born September 15, 1986<ref>{{cite web  url=https://twitter.com/Jenna_Marbles/status/643859770882002945  title=Jenna Mourey/Marbles on Twitter  work=Twitter}}</ref><ref>{{cite av media  url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nIsgoafssIo  title=Things I Get Excited About As An Adult  date=August 27, 2015  publisher=  via=YouTube}}</ref>), better known by her pseudonym Jenna Marbles, is an American YouTube personality YouTuber, vlogger, comedian, and actress. As of March 2018, her channel has over 2.6 billion video views and 18 million subscribers,<ref>{{cite av media  url=https://www.youtube.com/user/JennaMarbles  title=JennaMarbles  work=YouTube}}</ref> making it the List of most-subscribed YouTube channels 45th most subscribed channel on YouTube and the eighth most popular channel operated by a woman.<ref>{{Cite web  title=PewDiePie, Jenna Marbles, CaptainSparklez: The Webs Biggest Stars  work=Reason (magazine) Reason  date=October 1, 2014  accessdate=October 10, 2014  url=http://reason.com/archives/2014/10/01/pewdiepie-captainsparklez-skydoesminecra}}</ref> Marbles is the first social media star to have a wax figure displayed in Madame Tussauds Museum, located in New York City.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://mashable.com/2015/07/26/jenna-marbles-madame-tussauds/#pkWR4It0kZq1 title=Jenna Marbles, Smosh are first YouTube stars with their own wax figures last=Stark first=Chelsea work=Mashable access-date=October 26, 2017 language=en}}</ref>

==Early life==
Marbles was born and raised in Rochester, New York, where she graduated from Brighton High School (Rochester, New York) Brighton High School in 2004. Her father is a chemist who holds several patents and she has one older brother.<ref name="Draw My Life by JennaMarbles">{{cite news  first= last= title=Draw My Life- Jenna Marbles  url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sCxzaHC30Ec  work=JennaMarbles  publisher=  date=March 28, 2013  accessdate=March 28, 2013}}</ref> She then moved to Boston, where she attended Suffolk University. At Suffolk she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Psychology, and later attended Boston University for a Master of Education in Sport Psychology and Counseling.<ref name="variety">{{Cite web  title=YouTube Star Jenna Marbles: 5 Facts About Her Rise to Internet Fame  first=Todd  last=Spangler  work=Variety (magazine) Variety  date=July 18, 2013  accessdate=January 16, 2015  url=https://variety.com/2013/digital/news/youtube-star-jenna-marbles-5-facts-about-her-rise-to-internet-fame-1200564607/}}</ref>

==Career==
Marbles initially started her career with Barstool Sports, where she wrote for their female counterpart site StoolLaLa.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.nbcnews.com/id/53821689/ns/business-small_business/t/man-behind-bible-bro-culture/#.U5DQ8fldWBI  title=The Man Behind the Bible of Bro Culture  publisher=NBC News  date=December 13, 2013}}</ref>
In the summer of 2010, Marbles was sharing a three-bedroom apartment in Cambridge, Massachusetts. She paid $800 rent by working various jobs, like bartending, working at a tanning salon, blogging, and go-go dancing at nightclubs.<ref name="nytimes.com">{{cite web  url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/04/14/fashion/jenna-marbles.html  title=The Woman With 1 Billion Clicks: Jenna Marbles  date=April 14, 2013  work=The New York Times}}</ref>

Her pseudonym "Jenna Marbles" comes from her mothers complaint that "Mourey," their legal surname, pulled up only Jennas videos on Google. ===Other ventures===
Marbles released a brand of dog toys called Kermie Worm & Mr. Marbles. The toys appearances are based upon her real dogs.<ref name="variety" /> She has also created items with some of her most memorable quotes printed on them (e.g. "what are this").

Marbles also hosts a weekly pop countdown on SiriusXM Hits 1 called "YouTube 15".<ref>SiriusXM Hits 1</ref><ref>{{cite web  url=http://blog.siriusxm.com/tag/jenna-marbles/  title=jenna marbles&nbsp;– SiriusXM Blog  work=siriusxm.com}}</ref>

In 2016, Marbles became an executive producer for Maximum Ride (2016 film) Maximum Ride, a film based on the series of novels of the same name by James Patterson.<ref>{{cite web  url=https://m.imdb.com/title/tt0825283/  title=Maximum Ride (2016)  publisher=  accessdate=October 25, 2016}}</ref>

Marbles does a weekly podcast with her boyfriend Julien Solomita called "The Jenna Julien Podcast" (formerly called "The Jenna and Julien Podcast"). Topics focus on things such as stories of growing up, conspiracy theories, YouTubers and other general topics. They sometimes feature other YouTubers as guests on their podcast including Shane Dawson among others<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.youtube.com/user/JennaJulienPodcast title=Jenna Julien website=YouTube accessdate=May 13, 2017}}</ref>.
