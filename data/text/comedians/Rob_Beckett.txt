Robert Beckett (born 2 January 1986)<ref name=birthyear>{{Cite episode    title = Series 13, Episode 8    episodelink = List of Mock the Week episodes#Series 13   url = https://www.bbc.co.uk/iplayer/episode/b04hz9df/mock-the-week-series-13-episode-8    accessdate = 22 September 2014    network = BBC    date = 18 September 2014    series = Mock the Week    seriesno = 13    number = 8    time = 11:30}}</ref> is an English stand-up comedian and presenter. He was a co-host on the ITV2 spin-off show Im a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! NOW! from 2012 to 2014. In September 2015 it was confirmed that Beckett would not return to the show. Since 2016, Beckett has been a team captain on the E4 (TV channel) E4 panel show 8 Out of 10 Cats.

He presents BBC One entertainment series Wedding Day Winners and  All Together Now (UK TV series) All Together Now. He is also set to host Channel 4 video game show Rob Becketts Playing for Time in 2018.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.chortle.co.uk/news/2018/02/11/39119/rob_beckett_to_front_video_game_show title=Rob Beckett to front video game show : News 2018 :     Chortle : The UK Comedy Guide first=Powder Blue Internet Business last=Solutions website=www.chortle.co.uk}}</ref>

==Early life==
Beckett was born in Mottingham, London Mottingham, London.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.newsshopper.co.uk/news/10657646.Mottingham_comedian_Rob_Beckett_chats_to_Vibe_ahead_of_UK_tour/ title=Mottingham comedian Rob Beckett chats to Vibe ahead of UK tour author=Jim Palmer date=5 September 2013}}</ref> He has four brothers. He went to Coopers School in Chislehurst with comedian Tom_Allen_(comedian) Tom Allen .

==Career==
Beckett started performing stand-up in 2009 and quickly enjoyed success in all of the new comedian competitions winning four in his first year.{{cn date=October 2016}} His consistently impressive performances resulted in coming third in So You Think Youre Funny,<ref>{{Cite web url=http://soyouthinkyourefunny.co.uk/past-winners/sytyf-alumni/ title=Finalists & Runners up – So You Think Youre Funny? website=soyouthinkyourefunny.co.uk access-date=8 July 2017}}</ref> and winning the Amused Moose Laugh-Off which earned him an invitation to perform at the Adelaide Fringe Festival in Australia.<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.amusedmoose.com/amused-moose-comedy-awards/amused-moose-laugh-off-2010/ title=AmusedMoose &#124; Stand-up Comedy Soho Kings Cross Awards Courses website=amusedmoose.com accessdate=8 July 2017}}</ref> In Adelaide, Beckett was nominated for the best newcomer award.{{cn date=October 2016}} He made his debut at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival in 2012 with his solo show "Rob Becketts Summer Holiday".<ref>{{Cite news url=https://edinburghfestival.list.co.uk/article/43992-rob-becketts-summer-holiday/ title=Rob Becketts Summer Holiday date=3 August 2012 work=Edinburgh Festival accessdate=8 July 2017}}</ref>

In 2011, Beckett made a guest appearance as Mike in the Channel 4 series Fresh Meat (TV series) Fresh Meat.<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1954673/ title=Rob Beckett publisher=IMDb}}</ref>

From 2012 until 2014, Beckett co-hosted Im a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! NOW!, the ITV2 spin-off from the main show. He presented the show with Laura Whitmore and Joe Swash.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/showbiz/tv/im_a__celebrity/4620419/Comic-Rob-Beckett-replaces-Russell-Kane-in-Im-A-Celebrity-spin-off-show.html title=Comic Robs job is to Kane it in the Im A Celebrity jungle date=1 November 2012}}</ref> He announced his departure from the show in October 2015, to concentrate on his tour. He was replaced by David Morgan (comedian) David Morgan.

Beckett has presented the RockNRoll Football Sunday show on Absolute Radio since August 2014.<ref>{{cite web url=http://absoluteradio.co.uk/presenters/rob-beckett-38/ title=Absolute Radio – Presenter Page – Rob Beckett accessdate=1 November 2014}}</ref>

Alongside fellow comedian Ian_Smith_(comedian) Ian Smith and former footballer Jimmy Bullard, Beckett hosts UK television channel Dave (TV channel) Daves comedy-football podcast "The Magic Sponge" – an irreverent look at the lives of professional footballers.<ref>{{cite news   url=https://itunes.apple.com/gb/podcast/magic-sponge-jimmy-bullard/id1072130463?mt=2  title=The Magic Sponge }}</ref>

He narrated the 2016 E4 (TV channel) E4 series Celebs Go Dating.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://www.ok.co.uk/tv/1011962/celebs-go-dating-who-is-narrator-rob-beckett-viewers-love-stephen-bear-ferne-mccann-e4 title=Celebs Go Dating viewers make confession about hilarious narrator Rob Beckett last=Donaldson first=Laura date=24 February 2017 work=OK! access-date=9 July 2017}}</ref> In February 2017, the second series of Celebs Go Dating began airing with him writing and narrating once again, this time with some overseas celebrities in tow.  Since 2016, Beckett has been a team captain on the More4 panel show 8 Out of 10 Cats. Aisling Bea is the other team captain.

Beckett was a regular contestant and eventual winner on the third series of Taskmaster (TV series) Taskmaster, which aired on October 2016. From 2018, hes presented Wedding Day Winners, with Lorraine Kelly<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/mediacentre/latestnews/2017/wedding-day-winners?ns_mchannel=social&ns_campaign=bbc_press_office&ns_source=twitter&ns_linkname=corporate title=Lorraine Kelly and Rob Beckett say I do to BBC Ones Wedding Day Winners publisher=BBC}}</ref> and All Together Now with Geri Horner, both Saturday-night entertainment series for BBC One.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/mediacentre/latestnews/2017/all-together-now?ns_mchannel=social&ns_campaign=bbc_press_office&ns_source=twitter&ns_linkname=corporate title=Rob Beckett joins in with All Together Now on BBC One publisher=BBC}}</ref>
