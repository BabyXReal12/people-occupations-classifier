
Jessica Chaffin is an American actress, comedian, and writer best known as part of the comedy duo Ronna and Beverly with Jamie Denbo. She is also known for her recurring role as Coco Wexler on Nickelodeons Zoey 101 and appearing in the films Spy (2015 film) Spy and The Heat (film) The Heat. Chaffin starred as Marie Faldonado in the CBS sitcom Man with a Plan (TV series) Man With A Plan, opposite Matt LeBlanc.

==Early life==
Chaffin was born and raised in Newton, Massachusetts. Her father is from a Jewish family, and her mother, who is of Scottish and English ancestry, Conversion to Judaism converted to Judaism; Chaffin was raised Jewish.<ref>{{cite web last=Carley  first=Brennan  url=http://www.bostonmagazine.com/arts-entertainment/blog/2013/05/28/ronna-and-beverly-talk-performing-for-ben-affleck/  title=Ronna and Beverly Talk Performing for Ben Affleck  publisher=Boston Magazine  date=2013-05-28  accessdate=2017-02-09}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web url=http://www.thejc.com/arts/arts-features/110324/jewish-mother-comics-ronna-and-beverly-are-feeling-the-heat title=Jewish mother comics Ronna and Beverly are feeling The Heat last= first= date= website=www.thejc.com archive-url= archive-date= dead-url= access-date=2017-10-09}}</ref> Her brother, Joshua Chaffin, is a writer for the Financial Times.

==Career==
She is a regular performer at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in Los Angeles, often performing with her comedy partner Jamie Denbo.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://losangeles.ucbtheatre.com/talent/view/5156  title=Jessica Chaffin  publisher=Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre  accessdate=November 28, 2012}}</ref> She has done many guest voice appearances on Nickelodeon animated programs. From 2008 to 2009, she worked with Amy Poehler as a writer and voice actor on the animated series The Mighty B! playing several roles, including that of Portias cousin, Chelsea. Chaffin is also known for playing Coco Wexler, the girls dorm adviser on all four seasons of Nickelodeons Zoey 101.

Chaffin has made guest appearances on television programs such as Weeds (TV series) Weeds, Entourage (U.S. TV series) Entourage, and Curb Your Enthusiasm. In December 2009, her comedy pilot Ronna & Beverly (based on the popular stage show she performs with Denbo at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre) aired on Showtime (TV channel) Showtime. Ronna & Beverly was eventually turned into a six-part comedy talk show, which was co-produced by Paul Feig and premiered on September 10, 2012 on Sky Atlantic Sky Atlantic HD in the United Kingdom.<ref>{{cite web  last=Kemp  first=Stuart  url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/paul-feig-ronny-beverly-uk-tv-show-330369  title=Paul Feig Co-Producing Ronna & Beverly For Sky Atlantic  work=The Hollywood Reporter  publisher=Prometheus Global Media  date=May 29, 2012  accessdate=November 28, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web  url=http://skyatlantic.sky.com/ronna-beverly/bridesmaids-paul-feig-introduces-ronna-beverly  title=Bridesmaids Paul Feig Introduces Ronna & Beverly  publisher=Sky Atlantic Sky Atlantic HD  accessdate=November 28, 2012}}</ref> Together with Denbo, Chaffin created the Ronna and Beverly podcast.

Chaffin is formerly a series regular on the CBS sitcom Man with a Plan (TV series) Man With A Plan.
