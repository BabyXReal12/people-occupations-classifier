He has also acted in the films The Benchwarmers, School for Scoundrels (2006 film) School for Scoundrels, Blades of Glory, Mamas Boy, When in Rome (2010 film) When in Rome, and Walt Before Mickey as Roy O. Heder was born in Fort Collins, Colorado, the son of Helen (née Brammer) and Dr. James Heder, a practicing physician. He has an identical twin brother,
Dan, as well as an older sister and brother, Rachel and Doug, and two younger brothers, Adam and Matt. Heder is the nephew by marriage of former National Football League NFL running back and WCAU sportscaster, Vai Sikahema.
Heder is of part Swedish descent.
Heder and his parents moved to Salem, Oregon, when he was about two years old.
