
Affion Scott Crockett (born August 12, 1973) is an American actor, writer, dancer, rapper, comedian and music producer. He has appeared in many films and television shows.

==Early life==
Crockett was raised in France Germany and Fayetteville, North Carolina.<ref>http://str8nyc.com/exclusive-interviews/affion-crocket/</ref> He grew up in an Army family. One of Crocketts grandfathers was Chinese Trinidadian and Tobagonian Chinese Trinidadian.{{fact date=February 2015}}

He attended Fayetteville State University, graduating with a bachelors degree in business.

==Career==
Crockett appeared in HBOs Def Comedy Jam in 1996, demonstrating his impressions and physical comedy. He was a cast member of the game show Wild n Out, starring Nick Cannon.<ref name="hollywoodreporter1">{{cite web url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/mtv2-reviving-nick-cannons-wild-n-out-388448 title=MTV2 Reviving Nick Cannons Wild n Out (Exclusive) date=November 17, 2011 publisher=Hollywoodreporter.com accessdate=September 29, 2013}}</ref> He has appeared on Curb Your Enthusiasm, Blackish<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1649714/ title=Affion Crockett website=IMDb access-date=2017-12-07}}</ref>, co-starred in the films A Haunted House  A Haunted House 1 & A Haunted House 2  2 <ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1649714/ title=Affion Crockett website=IMDb access-date=2017-12-07}}</ref>with Marlon Wayans, Welcome Home Roscoe Jenkins with Martin Lawrence, and Soul Men with Samuel L. Jackson and Bernie Mac, The Wedding Ringer<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1649714/ title=Affion Crockett website=IMDb access-date=2017-12-07}}</ref> with Kevin Hart and Josh Gad, and Pixels<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1649714/ title=Affion Crockett website=IMDb access-date=2017-12-07}}</ref> with Adam Sandler.

Crockett starred in and produced his own sketch comedy show for Fox Broadcasting Company Fox called In the Flow with Affion Crockett which was executive produced by Jamie Foxx.<ref name="hollywoodreporter2">{{cite web url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1649714/?ref_=nv_sr_1 title=Affion Crockett IMDB page last= first= date=November 17, 2011 website= archive-url= archive-date= dead-url= accessdate=}}</ref> He showed his versatility when he wrote, produced, and rapped on his debut comedy album, Watch the Clone,<ref>{{cite web url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/watch-the-clone/id573571159  title=iTunes – Music – Watch the Clone by Affion Crockett  publisher=Itunes.apple.com  date=November 6, 2012  accessdate=September 29, 2013}}</ref> available on iTunes.
