Gillian MacLaren Jacobs
({{IPAc-en ˈ ɡ ɪ l i ə n}}; born October 19, 1982)
is an American actress. She is known for portraying Britta Perry on the NBC/Yahoo! Screen comedy series Community (TV series) Community and Mickey Dobbs on the Netflix series Love (TV series) Love. Jacobs had a recurring role as Mimi-Rose Howard on the fourth season of the HBO series Girls (TV series) Girls and has appeared in films such as Gardens of the Night (2008), The Lookalike (2014 film) The Lookalike (2014), Life Partners (2014), Hot Tub Time Machine 2 (2015), Dont Think Twice (2016), Brother Nature (film) Brother Nature (2016), Life of the Party (2018 film) Life of the Party (2018) and Ibiza (film) Ibiza (2018).
