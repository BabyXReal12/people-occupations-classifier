==Personal life==
Misha Collins was born in Boston, Massachusetts, to Richard Krushnic{{cn date=October 2016}} and Rebecca Tippens.<ref name="TV Guide">{{cite web title=Misha Collins url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/misha-collins/bio/259304 website=TV Guide publisher=CBS Interactive accessdate=July 11, 2014}}</ref> He was raised in an irreligious family.<ref>{{cite web date=November 18, 2008 title=Misha Collins is Supernaturals Dark Angel url=http://screenertv.com/news-features/misha-collins-is-supernaturals-dark-angel/ website=Screener (website) Screener publisher= accessdate=January 17, 2018 author=OHare, Kate}}</ref> He has said that his surname, Krushnic, "goes back six generations in Canada, and were not sure where they came from."<ref name=nmte1>{{cite web url=http://www.everyjoe.com/2008/09/24/entertainment/misha-collins-answers-our-top-3-qs/ title=Misha Collins Answers Our Top 3 Qs – EveryJoe author= date= work=EveryJoe}} "My mother went to Russia when she was in college. She had a boyfriend named Misha, and much to my fathers chagrin she named me after him. And the last name, Krushnic, goes back six generations in Canada, and were not sure where they came from—could be Russian or Ukrainian or Polish."</ref> His stage name, Collins, is his great-grandmothers maiden name.{{citation needed date=March 2016}}

Before acting, he interned for four months at the White House during the Clinton Administration in the Office of Presidential Personnel.<ref name=cw>{{cite web  url=http://www.cwtv.com/shows/supernatural/cast/misha-collins  title= Supernatural Cast Bios Misha Collins  publisher= The CW  accessdate=May 5, 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite news last1=Krushnic first1=Dmitri title=Lewinsky wasnt the only infatuated intern url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/1998-01-29/news/1998029053_1_white-house-lewinsky-house-interns accessdate=July 11, 2014 work=Baltimore Sun date=January 29, 1998}}</ref> Collins attended Greenfield Center School, Northfield Mount Hermon School and the University of Chicago, where he studied social theory.<ref>{{cite news title=Just try to find a stranger scavenger hunt than this url=http://www.recorder.com/home/8080850-95/just-try-to-find-a-stranger-scavenger-hunt-than-this accessdate=July 11, 2014 work=The Recorder (Massachusetts newspaper) The Recorder date=August 16, 2013 quote=GISHWHES was created by American actor Misha Collins, who attended Greenfield Center School and graduated from Northfield Mount Hermon School in 1992.}}</ref><ref>{{cite news last1=Hale first1=Mike title=Eternal Life, Thanks to Angels and Abs url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/04/20/arts/television/secrets-to-the-long-life-of-supernatural.html accessdate=July 11, 2014 work=The New York Times date=April 18, 2014}}</ref>

On October 6, 2001, Collins married Victoria Vantoch, in Maine. They have a son, West Anaximander Collins (born September 23, 2010), and a daughter, Maison Marie Collins (born September 25, 2012).<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.seattlepi.com/entertainment/tv/tvguide/article/Supernatural-s-Misha-Collins-Wife-Have-a-Baby-3902611.php accessdate=October 5, 2012 title=Supernaturals Misha Collins, Wife Have a Baby first=Liz last=Raftery publisher=TV Guide TV GUIDE date=September 28, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=https://twitter.com/mishacollins/status/251774579796758528 title=We got this on tuesday. No... accessdate=October 5, 2012 date=September 28, 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=http://www.celebritybabyscoop.com/2012/10/21/misha-collins-reveals-his-daughters-name  title=Misha Collins Reveals His Daughters Name  publisher=Celebritybabyscoop.com  date=October 21, 2012  accessdate=November 8, 2012}}</ref>

Collins is a published poet. His poems, including "Baby Pants" and "Old Bones" can be found in the 2008 edition of Columbia Poetry Review #21.

==Charity work==
Collins is the co-founder and board president of Random Acts,<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.randomacts.org/about/staff/ title=Random Acts – Our Staff author= date= work=Random Acts}}</ref> a non-profit organization dedicated to funding and inspiring acts of kindness around the world.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.randomacts.org/about/ title=Random Acts – Non-profit dedicated to acts of kindness worldwide author= date= work=Random Acts deadurl=yes archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150227091740/http://www.randomacts.org/about/ archivedate=February 27, 2015 df=mdy-all}}</ref> Collins founded the GISHWHES Greatest International Scavenger Hunt the World Has Ever Seen (GISHWHES) in 2011.<ref name=wired>{{cite news last=Mandy first=Chaos title=GISHWHES – International Scavenger Hunt url=https://www.wired.com/geekmom/2012/10/gishwhes/ accessdate=October 22, 2013 newspaper=Wired (magazine) Wired date=October 11, 2012}}</ref> Entrance fees for the international competition go towards Random Acts, and in 2012, GISHWHES broke the world record for most pledges to perform an act of kindness.<ref name=spec>{{cite news last=Hayes first=Molly title=Wanted: Rooster costume and Russian colonel url=http://www.thespec.com/news-story/4029952-wanted-rooster-costume-and-russian-colonel/ accessdate=October 22, 2013 newspaper=The Hamilton Spectator date=August 14, 2013}}</ref>
