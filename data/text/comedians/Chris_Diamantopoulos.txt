Christopher Diamantopoulos (born May 9, 1975; {{lang-el Χριστόφορος "Κρις" Διαμαντόπουλος }}) is a  Canadian actor and comedian.

==Career==
Diamantopoulos began appearing in TV commercials and professional theatre productions at the age of 9, and by the time he turned 18, he had left home to partake in a series of United States U.S. national tours.<ref>{{cite web  url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0224565/biotitle  title=Biography for Chris Diamantopoulos  accessdate=5 April 2012  publisher=IMDb}}</ref> A short time later, he worked on Broadway theatre Broadway where he played leading parts in The Full Monty (musical) The Full Monty (2002) and Les Misérables (musical) Les Misérables (2003).<ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.ibdb.com/person.php?id=91699  title=Chris Diamantopoulos  accessdate=11 April 2012  publisher=IBDB: The official source for Broadway Information}}</ref> Diamantopoulos attended East York Collegiate Institute.

He portrayed Robin Williams in Behind the Camera: The Unauthorized Story of Mork & Mindy alongside costar Erinn Hayes. He has guest-starred in several series, including Kevin Hill, Charmed, The Sopranos, Nip/Tuck, and Boston Legal. In 2007, he appeared opposite Debra Messing in The Starter Wife (miniseries) The Starter Wife, where he played the role of Rodney, the interior decorator. 

Diamantopoulos had a recurring role on State of Mind (TV series) State of Mind. He also guest-voiced on American Dad! in 2009.

On the eighth season of 24 (TV series) 24, he played President of the United States US President Allison Taylors White House Chief of Staff Chief of Staff Rob Weiss. He played Frank Sinatra in The Kennedys (miniseries) The Kennedys. Diamantopoulos played Moe Howard in the 2012 The Three Stooges (2012 film) The Three Stooges film. He also is the current spokesman for the firm Charles Schwab.<ref>sourced: ‘The Three Stooges’: The new Moe speaks for Charles Schwab posted by halboedeker on February 16, 2012 8:55 AM</ref> He understudied Donny Osmond on tour of Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat.

He had recurring roles on the NBC sitcoms Up All Night (TV series) Up All Night, as Maya Rudolphs on and off lover, Julian; The Office (U.S. TV series) The Office, as Brian, a former member of the shows documentary film crew; and on Arrested Development (TV series) Arrested Development, as Marky, Lindsays face-blind environmentalist boyfriend. He is also the voice of Mickey Mouse for the Mickey Mouse (TV series) eponymous animated series.<ref name="roto">{{cite web  url=http://www.therotoscopers.com/2013/06/25/disney-shorts-debut-with-new-voice-for-mickey-mouse/  title=Disney Shorts Debut with New Voice for Mickey Mouse  first=Blake  last=Taylor  work=The Rotoscopers  date=June 27, 2013  accessdate=June 27, 2013}}</ref>.

He has recently featured in the UK/US television sitcom Episodes (TV series) Episodes playing the part of TV network boss Castor Sotto, Mr. Chris in the TV adaptation of About a Boy (TV series) About a Boy, and billionaire investor Russ Hanneman in  Silicon Valley (TV series) Silicon Valley.

In 2017 Diamantopoulos starred in the musical Waitress (musical) Waitress on Broadway.

==Personal life==
Diamantopoulos grew up splitting his time between Canada and Greece. He is of Greeks Greek descent and is a practicing member of the Greek Orthodox Church Greek Orthodox church.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.undernewmanagement-themovie.com/cast.php  title=Under New Management - The Movie : Wall Street Meets Mob Street - Cast  publisher=Undernewmanagement-themovie.com  date=2009  accessdate=2014-02-22  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101111012450/http://www.undernewmanagement-themovie.com/cast.php  archivedate=2010-11-11  df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite news  url=https://pqasb.pqarchiver.com/greenvilleonline/access/2418899691.html?FMT=ABS&FMTS=ABS:FT&type=current&date=Aug+7%2C+2011&author=Lillia%20Callum-Penso&pub=The+Greenville+News&desc=Local+pair+puts+Book+of+Revelation+on+the+app&pqatl=google   work=The Greenville News  first=Lillia  last=Callum-Penso  title=Local pair puts Book of Revelation on the app  date=August 7, 2011}}</ref> He met his wife, actress Becki Newton, in a New York City Subway subway station in New York City New York.
