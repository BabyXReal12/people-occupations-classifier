Jeremy James Hardy (born 17 July 1961) is an English comedian.

Born and raised in North Hampshire, he studied at the University of Southampton and began his stand-up career in the 1980s, going on to win the Edinburgh Comedy Awards Perrier Comedy Award at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe in 1988. Today he is best known for his appearances on radio panel shows such as the The News Quiz News Quiz and Im Sorry I Havent a Clue.

==Early life==
Hardy was born in Farnborough, Hampshire Farnborough, Hampshire.  He attended Farnham College and studied Modern History and Politics at the University of Southampton. He subsequently attempted but failed  to obtain a place on a journalism course. He also considered being an actor or poet.
