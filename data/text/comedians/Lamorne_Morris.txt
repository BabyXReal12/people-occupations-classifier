Lamorne Morris (born August 14, 1983) is an American actor, comedian and television personality. He is best known for playing Winston Bishop in the Fox Broadcasting Company Fox sitcom New Girl. As a game show host, he hosted the Cartoon Network game show BrainRush. He also worked for Black Entertainment Television BET in the past.

==Life and career==
Morris is originally from Glen Ellyn, a suburb near Chicago. He initially wanted to be a basketball player. He attended Glenbard South High School. Morris graduated the College of DuPage in 2003,<ref>http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/lamorne-morris/bio/297723</ref> The Second City Training Center, and was a member of Second Citys Outreach And Diversity Ensemble. He made his TV debut as host of his own show HotWyred on BET, a witty interactive tech and gaming show. Morris has since become a familiar funny guy as one of BET’s most visible on-air personalities, with his comedic antics and coverage for many of the network’s top-rated shows. From signature skits like “The Package” leading up to the annual BET Awards to segments for 106 & Park, BET’s Spring Bling: Fashion 5-0, 106 & Party, Top 100 Countdown, Grammy Nomination Special, and many more. Most recently Morris has been seen as the co-host of the daily music and entertainment news show BET Now with Alesha Renee.

Morris has appeared in several award-winning independent films and shorts including April Fools (Code Black), One Word (Panel Select at Cannes Film Festival Cannes) and voice-over voiceover work for animated comedy Urban Group Squirrels.{{citation needed date=October 2013}} He studied theatre at the College of DuPage near Chicago and is a recipient of the Chris Farley Memorial Acting Scholarship. With his recent relocation from New York City New York to Los Angeles, Morris has begun developing several of his own show concepts and is up for several film and television projects.{{citation needed date=October 2013}}

Morris can be seen in commercials including ones for State Farm Insurance, Taco Bell, Twix, Miller Lite, Las Vegas Valley Las Vegas, 7 Up, Edge, Sears, Microsoft Windows, Chilis, McDonalds, Ford and Hornitos Tequila.

Morris is featured in the supporting role of Winston in the Zooey Deschanel anchored comedy series New Girl, which premiered September 20, 2011, on Fox Broadcasting Company Fox.<ref name=boston>{{cite news last=Wright first=Emily title=Recap: New Girl star Lamorne Morris chats about the show, more url=http://www.boston.com/ae/tv/blog/2013/09/new_girl_star_l.html publisher=Boston date=September 5, 2013}}</ref>  However, Morris did not appear until the second episode<ref>{{cite news  url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/new-girl-lamorne-morris-replaces-209270   work=The Hollywood Reporter   first=Lesley   last=Goldberg   title=New Girl: Lamorne Morris Replaces Damon Wayans Jr. in Fox Comedy   date=July 8, 2011}}</ref> - Originally, Damon Wayans Jr. was a member of the cast, in the supporting role of Coach. However, Wayans other comedy series, Happy Endings (TV series) Happy Endings, had already been picked up for a second season on rival network American Broadcasting Company ABC. The producers of New Girl initially planned to recast Wayans role,<ref>http://www.deadline.com/2011/05/abc-renews-happy-endings/</ref> but later decided not to recast and instead write his character out in the second episode.

Morris has also appeared in some YouTube videos by the channels "FunnyorDie" and, more recently, "Water". Morris featured in one of Waters videos starring Matt Damon which aim to increase awareness about the lack of water in developing countries. Also, Morris starred in a parody music video of the song "Wiggle (song) Wiggle" by Jason Derulo in the YouTube channel "Bart Baker" as Jason Derulo in August 2014.
