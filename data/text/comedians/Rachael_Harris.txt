Rachael Elaine Harris (born January 12, 1968) is an US American actress and comedian. She is known for her numerous television roles, such as starring as Dr. Linda Martin on Lucifer (TV series) Lucifer and as a guest star on TV shows such as Sister, Sister (TV series) Sister, Sister, Reno 911!, New Girl (U.S. TV series) New Girl, Suits (U.S. TV series) Suits and The Good Wife.

==Career==

===Stage===
Harris performed with the Los Angeles improvisational comedy troupe the Groundlings and taught for a time with the Groundlings school.
Off-Broadway, she performed in Love, Loss, and What I Wore.
===Television===
File:Rachael Harris 2 by David Shankbone.jpg right thumb Harris at the April 2007 Tribeca Film Festival.
She debuted on television in a 1993 episode of SeaQuest DSV. After an appearance on Star Trek: Voyager in 1997, Harris went on to a recurring role in The WBs Sister, Sister (TV series) Sister, Sister. Other television credits include a stint as a correspondent for The Daily Show The Daily Shows 2002–2003 season,
as well as guest roles on The Sarah Silverman Program, Reno 911!, The West Wing (television) The West Wing, The Good Guys (2010 TV series) The Good Guys, Friends, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Monk (TV series) Monk, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, and Desperate Housewives in November 2008. Harris played the supporting role of Kevyn Shecket, Kirstie Alleys personal makeup artist, on the Showtime (TV network) Showtime series Fat Actress in 2005.
She also appears in Suits (U.S. TV series) Suits as Sheila Sazs, Louis Litts on-and-off again love interest. She played Cooper in the ABC sitcom Notes from the Underbelly.

Harris hosted Smoking Gun TV in 2004, and has made multiple appearances on such VH1 documentaries as I Love the 80s (U.S. TV series) I Love the 80s, I Love the 90s (U.S. TV series) I Love the 90s, and Best Week Ever. On ABCs Cougar Town, she played Shanna, a woman that the network describes as being the "nemesis" of Jules.
Also on ABC, she made a guest appearance in the Modern Family episode "Caught in the Act (Modern Family) Caught in the Act" as a Amelia, a restaurateur and mother to a playmate of Lily Pritchett.

In March 2014, Harris began a new role in the very short-lived series Surviving Jack with Chris Meloni.
In March 2015, Harris was cast as Linda, Lucifers therapist in the FOX television series Lucifer (TV series) Lucifer.
===Feature films===
Her film credits include roles in Best in Show (film) Best in Show, A Mighty Wind, For Your Consideration (film) For Your Consideration, Kicking &amp; Screaming (2005 film) Kicking &amp; Screaming, and Daddy Day Care.
In the 2009 comedy The Hangover (film) The Hangover she plays Melissa, girlfriend to Ed Helmss character Stu. She previously co-starred with Helms on The Daily Show.
She plays the mother of the main character in the film Diary of a Wimpy Kid (2010) and its two sequels.

==Personal life==
Harris was born in Worthington, Ohio Worthington, Ohio. She graduated from Thomas Worthington High School Worthington High School in 1986 and Otterbein College in 1989, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts in theatre.
Harris and violinist Christian Hebel eloped in early 2015 and wed in New York City. On July 19, 2016, Harris gave birth to her first child, son Henry, with Hebel. On February 8th, 2018, on Live with Kelly and Ryan (from the Bahamas), Harris announced that she and Hebel are expecting their second child.
