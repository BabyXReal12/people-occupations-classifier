==Career==
While at Harvard, Jost won $5,250 on a college edition of Weakest Link (U.S. game show) Weakest Link. Shortly after graduating from Harvard, he was hired as a writer at National Broadcasting Company NBCs Saturday Night Live (SNL) in 2005<ref name=TVGuide/> at 22 years old. From 2009 to 2012 Jost was SNLs writing supervisor. He was co-head writer from 2012 to 2015 and regained that status in late 2017.<ref>https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2017/12/colin-jost-michael-che-head-writers-saturday-night-live</ref> He often collaborates with fellow SNL co-head writer Rob Klein.<ref>{{cite web  last=Gallagher  first=Brian  url=http://www.movieweb.com/news/tracy-morgan-set-for-id-theft  title=Tracy Morgan Set for ID Theft  publisher=MovieWeb  date=March 5, 2010}}</ref> During the summer hiatus following the 2012-13 season, executive producer Lorne Michaels asked Jost if he could do the "Weekend Update" feature,<ref name=TVGuide/> as co-anchor Seth Meyers would soon be leaving to host Late Night With Seth Meyers. Jost accepted and replaced Meyers on the March 1, 2014, episode.<ref>{{cite web  url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/snl-names-new-weekend-update-673533  title=SNL Names New Weekend Update Co-Anchor  first=Lesley  last=Goldberg  work=The Hollywood Reporter  date=January 23, 2014  accessdate=January 23, 2014}}</ref> Jost names Norm Macdonald as a primary influence for his "Update" anchor work, as Macdonalds tone was the one Jost grew up with in high school. He also names Tina Fey as an influence.<ref name=TVGuide/> In addition to Weekend Update, Jost made a brief cameo appearance as Ohio Governor John Kasich in a Republicanism in the United States Republican presidential debate sketch.<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live/cast/colin-jost-14796/impersonation/john-kasich-210801 title=Watch John Kasich Sketches From SNL Played By Colin Jost - NBC.com website=NBC access-date=2016-07-02}}</ref>

As a stand-up comedian, Jost has appeared on Late Night With Jimmy Fallon as well as on TBS (U.S. TV channel) TBS and HBO.<ref>{{cite web last=Cavin first=Cory url=http://www.latenightwithjimmyfallon.com/blogs/2011/01/check-out-comedian-colin-jost-tonight/ title=Check Out Comedian Colin Jost Tonight! work=Late Night with Jimmy Fallon date=January 28, 2011}}</ref> He was selected as a "New Face" at the Montréal Just for Laughs festival in 2009,<ref>{{cite web  last=Gadino  first=Dylan P.  url=http://www.laughspin.com/2010/07/16/just-for-laughs-feimster-lawrence-jost-impress-at-new-faces/  archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130426110556/http://www.laughspin.com/2010/07/16/just-for-laughs-feimster-lawrence-jost-impress-at-new-faces/  dead-url=yes  archive-date=April 26, 2013  title=Just For Laughs: Feimster, Lawrence, Jost impress at New Faces  publisher=LaughSpin  date=July 16, 2010 }}</ref> and has since appeared several times at the Chicago Just for Laughs festival (2011 and 2012) and the Montréal festival (2010 and 2012). Jost has published three "Shouts and Murmurs" pieces in The New Yorker magazine, in addition to writing for The New York Times Magazine, The Huffington Post, The Staten Island Advance and Radar (magazine) Radar.<ref>{{cite web  last=Jost  first=Colin  url=http://www.newyorker.com/humor/2011/04/04/110404sh_shouts_jost  title=Explaining Your Time Warner Bill  work=The New Yorker  date=April 4, 2011}}</ref>
Jost wrote the screenplay of and played a minor role in the 2015 comedy film Staten Island Summer. He also had a minor role as Paul in the 2016 romcom feature How to Be Single.<ref>{{Cite news  url=http://www.silive.com/entertainment/tvfilm/index.ssf/2016/01/watch_colin_jost_dump_alison_b.html  title=Watch Colin Jost dump Alison Brie in How to be Single trailer}}</ref>

==Personal life==
It was confirmed in December 2017 that Jost had begun a relationship with actress Scarlett Johansson.<ref>{{Cite news url=https://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/scarlett-johansson-colin-jost-red-carpet-photos_us_5a21787be4b0a02abe90d1ee title=Introducing ScarJost: Scarlett Johansson And Colin Jost Go Public last=Sieczkowski first=Cavan date=2017-12-01 work=Huffington Post access-date=2018-05-20 language=en-US}}</ref>
