Jared Antonio Farrow, better known by his stage name Jay Pharoah, is an American actor, stand-up comedian, impressionist (entertainment) impressionist, and rapper. He joined the cast of NBCs Saturday Night Live in 2010, for its Saturday Night Live (season 36) thirty-sixth season.
In 2015, he was ranked the 55th greatest Saturday Night Live cast member by Rolling Stone magazine.

Pharoah began doing impersonations at age six and cites Gilbert Gottfrieds character in Aladdin (1992 Disney film) Aladdin, Iago (Aladdin) Iago, as his first voice, explaining, "My father put me in a talent competition a couple of months later, and out of the whole thing I got fifth place."
