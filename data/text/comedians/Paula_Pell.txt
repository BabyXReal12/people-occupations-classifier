Paula Pell (born April 15, 1963) is an United States American comedy writer, producer, and actress, best known for her work writing for the sketch series Saturday Night Live.
== Life and career ==

Pell has been a writer for Saturday Night Live since 1995. She also was a producer and writer for the sitcom 30 Rock, and is credited for writing the episodes Argus (30 Rock) "Argus" and Floyd (30 Rock) "Floyd". In 2006, a pilot she wrote, Thick and Thin, was picked up by NBC for 13 episodes, with Pell as executive producer.
