Fereydun Robert "Fred" Armisen<ref name=phawk1>{{cite web url=http://www.phawker.com/2014/05/08/excerpt-fredlandia/ title=Fredlandia: The Nicest Punk In Show Biz date=May 8, 2014 author=Phawker access-date=September 1, 2015}}</ref> (born December 4, 1966)<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.tvguide.com/celebrities/fred-armisen/bio/196439 title=Fred Armisen profile publisher=TVGuide.com accessdate=March 10, 2014}}</ref> is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer, and musician. Armisen founded ThunderAnt.com, a website that features the comedy sketches created with Brownstein, and is the bandleader for the Late Night with Seth Meyers house band, The 8G Band.

==Career==

===Music===
In 1984, Armisen played drums in a local band along with his high school friends in Valley Stream, New York, but the group soon ended. In 1988, he moved to Chicago to play drums for the punk rock band Trenchmouth,<ref>{{cite web last=DeRogatis  first=Jim  url=http://www.jimdero.com/News2003/FredArmisenFeb5.htm  title=All Ke-e-e-ding Aside  publisher=Jimdero.com  date=February 5, 2003  accessdate=January 21, 2012}}</ref> and in the 1990s, he played background drums with Blue Man Group.

Armisen played drums on three tracks for Les Savy Favs 2007 album Lets Stay Friends,<ref>{{cite web author=Jason Lymangrover  url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/lets-stay-friends-mw0000748733  title=Lets Stay Friends – Les Savy Fav &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits, Awards  publisher=AllMusic  date=September 18, 2007  accessdate=2014-02-11}}</ref> as well as tracks for Matthew Sweets 2011 album Modern Art (Matthew Sweet album) Modern Art<ref name="wanderinglucy.bandcamp.com">{{cite web author=  url=https://wanderinglucy.bandcamp.com/album/leap-year-klp053  title=Leap Year (KLP053) &#124; Wandering Lucy  website=Wanderinglucy.bandcamp.com  date=2015-07-06  accessdate=2017-01-24}}</ref> and Wandering Lucys 2015 album Leap Year.<ref name="wanderinglucy.bandcamp.com"/>

Armisen is the bandleader of the 8G Band, the house band for Late Night with Seth Meyers, as of February 24, 2014.<ref>{{cite web last=Murphy  first=Samantha  url=http://mashable.com/2014/02/10/fred-armisen-band-leader-seth-meyers/  title=Fred Armisen Joins Late Night with Seth Meyers as Bandleader  publisher=Mashable.com  date=November 18, 2011  accessdate=2014-02-11}}</ref>
