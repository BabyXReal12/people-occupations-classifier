Timothy Aaron "Tim" Hawkins (born March 30, 1968) is an American Christian comedian, songwriter, and singer, best known for parodying popular songs such as Carrie Underwoods "Jesus, Take The Wheel", Kansas (band) Kansass "Dust in the Wind", and "The Candy Man", along with stand-up material based on topics such as marriage, homeschooling, and parenting.<ref name=news>{{cite web  last=Krieg  first=Sheryl  title=Tim Hawkins finds the funny in everyday living  url=http://www.news-sentinel.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20121003/ENT/310039988/1009/LIVING  work=The News-Sentinel  accessdate=November 3, 2014  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141103025007/http://www.news-sentinel.com/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=%2F20121003%2FENT%2F310039988%2F1009%2FLIVING  archivedate=November 3, 2014  df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=http://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/story/entertainment/2017/07/11/tims-take-share-some-laughs-hawkins-mpac/467623001/ title=Tims take: Share some laughs with Hawkins at MPAC publisher=}}</ref><ref>{{cite web url=http://www.modbee.com/entertainment/article78395562.html title=Tim Hawkins brings good, clean fun to Gallo Center publisher=}}</ref> Hawkins has released five CDs and nine DVDs since 2002. Hawkins began to release his comedy on the Internet, with his videos gaining more than 200 million views on YouTube, GodTube, and Facebook as of early 2013.<ref name=crosswalk>{{cite web last=Hatcher first=Lori title=Tim Hawkins - Homeschooling Is Something to Laugh About url=http://www.crosswalk.com/family/homeschool/encouragement/tim-hawkins-homeschooling-is-something-to-laugh-about.html website=Crosswalk.com publisher=Salem Web Network accessdate=November 3, 2014}}</ref>
