Jimmy Brogan (born September 18, 1948), sometimes credited as Jim Brogan, is an American stand-up comedian, writer and actor.  He has made numerous standup appearances on the talk show circuit including The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson and Late Night with David Letterman.
He was a writer on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno for 9 years.
As an actor, he starred in the ABC sitcom Out of the Blue (1979 TV series) Out of the Blue.
