
Mike Birbiglia (born June 20, 1978) is an American comedian, actor, and filmmaker. He is a frequent contributor to This American Life and The Moth, and has released several comedy albums and television specials. His feature-length directorial debut Sleepwalk with Me (2012), based on his one-man show of the same name and in which he also starred, won awards at the Sundance Film Festival Sundance and Nantucket Film Festival Nantucket film festivals. He also wrote, directed, and starred in the comedy-drama Dont Think Twice (2016). His 2010 book Sleepwalk with Me and Other Painfully True Stories was a The New York Times Best Seller list New York Times bestseller and a finalist for the 2011 Thurber Prize for American Humor.  Birbiglia has appeared in films such as Your Sisters Sister (2011), Cedar Rapids (film) Cedar Rapids (2011), and Trainwreck (film) Trainwreck (2015), played a recurring role in Orange Is the New Black, and has guest starred in episodes of Girls (TV series) Girls, Inside Amy Schumer, and Broad City.

==Personal life==
Birbiglia has rapid eye movement sleep behavior disorder, which once caused him to run out of a second-story window of a motel he was staying in Walla Walla, Washington, while on tour. As a result of the accident, Birbiglia received 33 stitches in his leg.<ref name="Fresh Air 2010">{{cite web url=https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=130644070 title=Spending The Night With Sleepwalker Mike Birbiglia date=October 18, 2010 work=Fresh Air publisher=WHYY-FM WHYY accessdate=October 27, 2010 deadurl=no archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101031165644/http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=130644070 archivedate=October 31, 2010 df=mdy-all}}</ref><ref name="acp">{{cite web work=The Adam Carolla Show (podcast) The Adam Carolla Show  format=podcast  url=http://www.adamcarolla.com/ACPBlog/2010/10/14/mike-birbiglia  date=October 14, 2010  title=Mike Birbiglia  accessdate=October 27, 2010  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20101018041814/http://www.adamcarolla.com/ACPBlog/2010/10/14/mike-birbiglia/  archivedate=October 18, 2010  df= }}</ref><ref name=WE>{{cite news  url=http://washingtonexaminer.com/comedian-mike-birbiglia-returns-to-the-scene-of-the-crime/article/22126  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131123004921/http://washingtonexaminer.com/comedian-mike-birbiglia-returns-to-the-scene-of-the-crime/article/22126  archivedate=November 23, 2013  title=Comedian Mike Birbiglia returns to the scene of the crime  last=Klimek first=Chris  date=October 9, 2009  work=Washington Examiner  deadurl=no  accessdate=November 23, 2013}}</ref>
