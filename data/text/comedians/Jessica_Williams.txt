Jessica Renee Williams (born July 31, 1989) is an American actor actress and comedian who was formerly a senior List of The Daily Show correspondents correspondent on The Daily Show.

==Early life==
Jessica Renee Williams was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. She attended Narbonne High School Nathaniel Narbonne High School where she flourished in the schools drama department. She made her television debut as a series regular on the Nickelodeon series Just for Kicks (TV series) Just for Kicks in 2006<ref name="bosg060407"/> and in 2012, she became The Daily Show The Daily Shows youngest correspondent ever.<ref name="goth120119"/><ref>{{cite web  first=Elise  last=Czajkowski  publisher=Splitsider  title=Talking to Daily Show Correspondent Jessica Williams  url=http://splitsider.com/2014/02/talking-to-daily-show-correspondent-jessica-williams/  date=February 27, 2014  accessdate=December 2, 2014 }}</ref> She attended California State University, Long Beach.<ref name="ucb16019"/>  The results of a Genetic genealogy DNA test traced Williams maternal ancestry to the Bamileke people of Cameroon.<ref>[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FswIJqYOew8.html The Daily Show Correspondents Discover Their African Ancestry]</ref>

She currently co-hosts the comedy podcast 2 Dope Queens with Phoebe Robinson.
