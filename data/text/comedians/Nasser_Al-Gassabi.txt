Nasser Qassim Al Qasabi ({{lang-ar ناصر قاسم القصبي}}) is a Saudi Arabian actor. He started his acting career in 1984 and is known for his various roles in the series Tash Ma Tash No Big Deal (Arabic: طاش ما طاش
) In 2012, Nasser became one of the three judges on the television series, Arabs Got Talent.

Al Qasbi has featured in a Saudi TV show satirising ISIL called Selfie.
