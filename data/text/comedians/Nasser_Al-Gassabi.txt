
Nasser Qassim Al Qasabi ({{lang-ar ناصر قاسم القصبي}}) is a Saudi Arabian actor. He started his acting career in 1984 and is known for his various roles in the series Tash Ma Tash No Big Deal (Arabic: طاش ما طاش<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.waleg.com/archives/017438.html title=The Return of Tash 16 on MBC date=August 11, 2009 work=Waleg accessdate=25 May 2011}}</ref>) In 2012, Nasser became one of the three judges on the television series, Arabs Got Talent.

Al Qasbi has featured in a Saudi TV show satirising ISIL called Selfie.<ref>{{cite web url=http://edition.cnn.com/2015/06/22/middleeast/anti-isis-satire/index.html title=Saudi TV series deploys new weapon against ISIS: satire work=CNN date=22 Jun 2015}}</ref>
