James Adam Belushi ({{IPAc-en b ə ˈ l uː ʃ i}}; born June 15, 1954) is an Albanian-American actor, voice actor, comedian, singer and musician.
He is the younger brother of comic actor John Belushi. He is the father of actor Robert Belushi. He played the role of Jim on the sitcom According to Jim (2001–2009). His other television roles include Saturday Night Live (1983–1985), Wild Palms (1993), Aaahh!!! Real Monsters (1994–1997), Show Me a Hero (2015) and Twin Peaks (2017 TV series) Twin Peaks (2017).

Belushi has appeared in films such as Thief (film) Thief (1981), Trading Places (1983), About Last Night (1986 film) About Last Night (1986), Salvador (film) Salvador (1986), Red Heat (1988), K-9 (film) K-9 (1989), Mr. Destiny (1990), Curly Sue (1991), Jingle All the Way (1996), Underdog (film) Underdog (2007) and The Ghost Writer (film) The Ghost Writer (2010).
