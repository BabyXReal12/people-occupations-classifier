Bobby Mair is a Canadian stand-up comedian based in London, England who has appeared on The Hour (Canadian TV series) The Hour, Russell Howards Good News, Sweat the Small Stuff, Virtually Famous, Never Mind the Buzzcocks, Sam Delaneys News Thing and 8 Out of 10 Cats. Mair won the Laughing Horse New Act Of The Year competition in 2012.
He has supported the likes of Jerry Sadowitz, Doug Stanhope and Bill Burr.
