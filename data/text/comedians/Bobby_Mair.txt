Bobby Mair is a Canadian stand-up comedian based in London, England who has appeared on The Hour (Canadian TV series) The Hour, Russell Howards Good News, Sweat the Small Stuff, Virtually Famous, Never Mind the Buzzcocks, Sam Delaneys News Thing and 8 Out of 10 Cats. Mair won the Laughing Horse New Act Of The Year competition in 2012.<ref>{{cite web  url= http://www.chortle.co.uk/comics/b/33861/bobby_mair  title=Bobby Mair, comedian : Chortle : The UK Comedy Guide  first=  last=  work=chortle.co.uk  year=2014  quote=won the Laughing Horse New Act Of The Year competition in 2012.  accessdate=13 March 2014}}</ref> He has supported the likes of Jerry Sadowitz, Doug Stanhope and Bill Burr.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.chortle.co.uk/shows/edinburgh_fringe_2012/m/20882/michelle_de_swarte_&_bobby_mair:_unbalanced title=Michelle de Swarte & Bobby Mair: Unbalanced :     Chortle : The UK Comedy Guide first=Powder Blue Internet Business last=Solutions website=www.chortle.co.uk}}</ref>
