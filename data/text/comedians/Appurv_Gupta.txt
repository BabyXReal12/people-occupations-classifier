Appurv Gupta is an India Indian Stand-up comedy stand up comedian, Hinglish stand-up comedian and a satirist from India.<ref name="ibn">{{cite web title=20 Indian stand-up comedians to watch out for in 2014 url=http://ibnlive.in.com/news/20-indian-standup-comedians-to-watch-out-for-in-2014/445086-79.html accessdate=16 August 2014}}</ref><ref name=metro/><ref>{{cite web title=Professionals make a laughing matter of their jobs url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/life-style/relationships/work/Professionals-make-a-laughing-matter-of-their-jobs/articleshow/22114264.cms accessdate=28 August 2014}}</ref>

Gupta is a graduate of Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida in Bachelor of Technology. He completed his engineering in 2012 and chose to be a stand-up comedian rather than working for corporate offices.<ref name=metro>{{cite web title=Its a fun-filled show time! url=http://epaper.metroindia.com/story.aspx?id=4813&boxid=98332216&ed_date=2014-5-29&ed_code=820009&ed_page=13#.U4bfFCp3AfY.gmail accessdate=16 August 2014}}</ref> He has done around 1000 shows since then, including corporate performances for Bharti Airtel Airtel, Tanishq, Adobe Systems Adobe, American Express Amex and Radio Mirchi.<ref name=deccan>{{cite web title=City boy tickles the Capitals funny bone with clean humour url=http://www.deccanherald.com/content/381690/city-boy-tickles-capital039s-funny.html accessdate=16 August 2014}}</ref> His most applauded act is Appurview - Laugh with an Engineer which describes life from the point-of-view of an engineer.<ref name=toi>{{cite web title=Techie-turned-comic shuns stage cliches url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/gurgaon/Techie-turned-comic-shuns-stage-cliches/articleshow/32515801.cms accessdate=16 August 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web title=There is no vulgarity in my shows: Appurv Gupta url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/kannada/theatre/There-is-no-vulgarity-in-my-shows-Appurv-Gupta/articleshow/33182128.cms accessdate=16 August 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web title=Engineering humour not designs url=http://millenniumpost.in/NewsContent.aspx?NID=54716 accessdate=28 August 2014}}</ref>

Gupta was ranked second in "Top 20 Indian stand-up comedian in 2014" by CNN-IBN,<ref name="ibn"/> and was shortlisted in the Forbes Celebrity 100 Forbes India 100 Celebrity nominees list of 2015 by Forbes India.<ref>{{cite news title=Forbes India Celebrity 100 Nominees List for 2015 url=http://forbesindia.com/article/special/forbes-india-celebrity-100-nominees-list-for-2015/41475/1 accessdate=17 December 2015 work=Forbes India date=4 November 2015}}</ref>
