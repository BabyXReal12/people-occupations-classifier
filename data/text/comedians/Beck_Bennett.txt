
Christopher Beck Bennett (born October 1, 1984) is an American actor, writer, and comedian who is a current cast member on Saturday Night Live Saturday Night Live, joining in Saturday Night Live (season 39) 2013.<ref>{{Cite news url=http://splitsider.com/2013/08/here-are-your-new-snl-featured-players/ title=Here Are Your New ‘SNL’ Featured Players date=2013-08-23 work=Splitsider access-date=2018-01-21 language=en-US}}</ref> Before his tenure on SNL, he was recognized for the AT&T "Its Not Complicated" commercials where he interviewed kids,<ref>{{cite web last=Nudd  first=Tim  url=http://www.adweek.com/news/advertising-branding/how-att-got-kids-make-some-years-best-ads-148605  title=How AT&T Got Kids to Make Some of the Years Best Ads  publisher=Adweek  date=2013-04-15  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> his sketch videos with the comedy group Good Neighbor,<ref>{{cite web last=Forde  first=Kevin  url=http://www.cracked.com/blog/the-8-best-internet-sketch-troupes-whose-initials-arent-tam/  title=The 8 Best Internet Sketch Troupes Whose Initials Arent TAM  publisher=Cracked.com  date=  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web last=Perkins  first=Colin  url=http://splitsider.com/2010/10/sketch-artists-good-neighbor/  title=Sketch Artists: Good Neighbor Adds Some Absurdity to Viral Videos  publisher=Splitsider  date=2010-10-05  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web author=  url=http://www.g4tv.com/videos/35689/good-neighbor-sketch-comedy/  title=Good Neighbor Sketch Comedy  publisher=G4tv.com  date=2008-12-19  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> and his YouTube show Theatre of Life.<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NAqZ-GAVaz0 title=More Theatre of Life last=beckbennett first= date=2011-01-30 website=YouTube archive-url= archive-date= dead-url= access-date=2017-06-04}}</ref>

==Early life==
Bennett was born in Wilmette, Illinois (a suburb of Chicago), the son of Sarah and Andy Bennett. He performed in multiple shows at Childrens Theatre of Winnetka.<ref>{{cite web url=http://childrenstheatrewinnetka.com title=Childrens Theatre of Winnetka website=Childrens Theatre of Winnetka}}</ref> He graduated from New Trier High School in 2003 where he played Jean Valjean in his schools production of Les Misérables (musical) Les Misérables.<ref name="chicagotribune1">{{cite news url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2013-01-30/entertainment/ct-ent-0131-att-ads-beck-bennett-20130130_1_att-ads-little-kids  title=Beck Bennetts calling answered with AT&T ads - Chicago Tribune  publisher=Articles.chicagotribune.com  date=2013-01-30  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> Beck attended the USC School of Dramatic Arts in the B.F.A. Acting program.<ref name="chicagotribune1"/>

==Career==
In 2003, Bennett joined the improv/sketch group Commedus Interruptus along with Kyle Mooney and Nick Rutherford. In 2007, after graduation, Mooney, Rutherford, and Bennett joined with editor/director Dave McCary to form the sketch group Good Neighbor.<ref name="chicagotribune1"/> The group received praise from comedian Louis C.K.<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.reddit.com/r/IAmA/comments/tmlnp/louis_ck_reddit/c4nwatc  title=codythebeau comments on Louis C.K. reddit  publisher=Reddit.com  date=2012-05-14  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> and director Steven Spielberg, who sent Mooney, Bennett, Rutherford, and McCary a personal acknowledgement after being impressed with the Hook (film) Hook parody, Unbelievable Dinner. He complimented the sketch and encouraged the group to continue making funny movies.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.schubas.com/Mobile/Show+Details?showId=15276  title=Schubas  publisher=Schubas  date=2012-01-06  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite web last=Sykes  first=Tom  url=http://www.thedailybeast.com/articles/2013/04/09/beck-bennett-meet-that-guy-in-those-adorable-at-t-commercials.html  title=Beck Bennett: Meet That Guy in Those Adorable AT&T Commercials  publisher=The Daily Beast  date=2013-04-09  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref>

In 2011, Bennett created the satirical political talk show Fresh Perspectives,<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/04/07/fresh-perspective-libya-kids-video_n_846345.html  title=Fresh Perspective On Libya Offered By Adorable Kids (VIDEO) publisher=Huffingtonpost.com  date= 2011-04-07 accessdate=2013-10-16  first=Katla  last=McGlynn}}</ref><ref>{{cite web last=Hampton  first=Adele  url=http://thehill.com/video/campaign/219231-a-7-year-olds-take-on-mitt-romneys-candidacy  title=A seven-year-olds take on Mitt Romneys candidacy - The Hills Video  publisher=Thehill.com  date=2012-03-30  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref><ref>{{cite news url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/07/01/fresh-perspective-kids-talk-medicare_n_888735.html  title=Fresh Perspectives Kids Talk Medicare, Call Paul Ryan A Lame Doo Doo (VIDEO)  publisher=Huffingtonpost.com  date=2011-07-01  accessdate=2013-10-16  first=Christine  last=Friar}}</ref> which helped him land his AT&T commercials interviewing children.<ref>{{cite news url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/07/03/business/media/a-spokesman-finds-fame-interviewing-tiny-experts.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0 author=Stuart Elliott newspaper=The New York Times title=A Spokesman Finds Fame Interviewing Tiny Experts accessdate=2013-10-16 date=2013-07-02}}</ref>

In 2012, he appeared in the film Kill Me Now,<ref>{{cite web url=http://hex19.com/blog/kill-me-now-internet-sketch-comics-make-a-movie.html  title=Kill Me Now: Internet Sketch Comics Make A Movie - Geekblog - Hex19 &#124; Celebrating All Things Geek  publisher=Hex19  date=2012-07-26  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> a film starring multiple internet sketch-comedy groups, and filmed Beside Still Waters (film) Beside Still Waters<ref>{{cite news author=Jeff Sneider  url=https://variety.com/2012/film/news/chris-lowell-sets-feature-directorial-debut-1118054877/  title=Chris Lowell sets feature directorial debut newspaper=Variety (magazine) Variety date=2012-05-31  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> by writer/director Chris Lowell.

In March 2013, Bennett appeared in an episode of Last Man Standing (U.S. TV series) Last Man Standing, and that August voiced a character for Animation Domination FOX ADHDs Axe Cop (TV series) Axe Cop. That same year Good Neighbor produced a pilot for Comedy Central with Adam McKays Gary Sanchez Productions Gary Sanchez production company, though it was not picked up as a series, in light of the move of three of the troupes four members to New York City to star on Saturday Night Live.<ref name="splitsider1">{{cite web url=http://splitsider.com/2013/08/good-neighbor-is-the-new-lonely-island-snl-hires-director-dave-mccary-alongside-two-cast-members/ title=Good Neighbor Is the New Lonely Island: SNL Hires Director Dave McCary Alongside Two Cast Members last=Frucci first=Adam date=2013-08-23 publisher=Splitsider accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> That same year Bennett completed work on the independent film Balls Out (2014 film) Balls Out<ref>{{cite web url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2G20709KbfM  title=Intramural: The Movie - 5 Questions w/ Beck Bennett  publisher=YouTube  date=2013-08-01  accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref> and appeared in season 4 of Arrested Development (season 4) Arrested Development.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.uproxx.com/tv/2013/05/heres-every-single-arrested-development-season-4-guest-star/ title=Arrested Development Season 4 Guest Star List last=Kurp first=Josh date=2013-05-08 publisher=Uproxx.com accessdate=2013-10-16}}</ref>

Bennett voices Launchpad McQuack in the 2017 DuckTales (2017 TV series) DuckTales reboot.<ref>{{cite web url=http://screenrant.com/ducktales-reboot-2017-preview-images/ title=DuckTales Reboot Teaser Confirms Summer 2017 Premiere Date date=7 December 2016 publisher=}}</ref>

==Personal life==
On May 13, 2016, Bennett posted to his Instagram a photograph of himself with his girlfriend Jessy Hodges, in which the caption stated that the couple were celebrating five years with each other.<ref>{{Cite web url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BFU7ZM3gVOg/?hl=en&taken-by=beckbennett title=Instagram post by @beckbennett • May 13, 2016 at 12:17am UTC website=Instagram language=en access-date=2018-01-28}}</ref> He has been friends with fellow SNL cast member Kyle Mooney since they were students at the University of Southern California.<ref>{{Cite news url=https://dailytrojan.com/2011/01/23/student-comedy-group-grabs-attention/ title=Student comedy group grabs attention  {{!}} Daily Trojan date=2011-01-23 work=Daily Trojan access-date=2018-01-21 language=en-US}}</ref>
