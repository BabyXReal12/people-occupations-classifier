Rondell Jerome Sheridan (born August 15, 1958) is an American actor, comedian and television director, best known for his five-year portrayal of Victor Baxter, the goofy-yet-lovable father of a psychic teenager, in the Disney Channel sitcoms Thats So Raven and Cory in the House. Sheridan is an alumnus of Marquette University.
