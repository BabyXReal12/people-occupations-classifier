
Joshua David Duhamel ({{IPAc-en d ə ˈ m ɛ l}}; born November 14, 1972) is an American actor and former fashion model. After various modeling work, he made his acting debut as Leo du Pres on the American Broadcasting Company ABC daytime soap opera All My Children and later starred as Danny McCoy on NBCs Las Vegas (TV series) Las Vegas. 

Duhamel has ventured into film, appearing as one of the main protagonists in four of the Transformers (film series) Transformers films, most recently in the fifth entry, Transformers: The Last Knight (2017). He has also appeared in When in Rome (2010 film) When in Rome (2010), Life as We Know It (film) Life as We Know It (2010), New Years Eve (2011 film) New Years Eve (2011), Safe Haven (film) Safe Haven (2013), and Youre Not You (2014). In 2015, Duhamel co-starred on the short-lived CBS crime drama Battle Creek (TV series) Battle Creek. He also starred in two video games, most notably Call of Duty: WWII (2017). In 2018, he appeared in the romantic comedy-drama film Love, Simon.

==Early life==
Duhamel was born in Minot, North Dakota. His mother, Bonnie L. Kemper, is a retired teacher and local businesswoman, and his father, Larry Duhamel, is an advertising salesman.<ref name="Bio">{{cite web title=Josh Duhamel profile at FilmReference.com publisher=FilmReference.com url=http://www.filmreference.com/film/64/Josh-Duhamel.html accessdate=September 20, 2008}}</ref><ref name=ega/> He is of French Canadian Americans French-Canadian, Irish Americans Irish, English Americans English, Austrian Americans Austrian, German Americans German and Norwegian Americans Norwegian ancestry.<ref name=ega>{{cite web url=http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~battle/celeb/duhamel.htm title=RootsWeb.com Home Page website=freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com}}</ref> Duhamel is an active Catholic.<ref>{{cite web url=http://www.okmagazine.com/2011/05/josh-duhamel-is-a-strong-man-in-tights-on-ellen-degeneres  title=Josh Duhamel Is a (Strong) Man in Tights on ‘Ellen DeGeneres’  publisher=OK!  accessdate=September 5, 2011  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110721123809/http://www.okmagazine.com/2011/05/josh-duhamel-is-a-strong-man-in-tights-on-ellen-degeneres/  archivedate=July 21, 2011  df=mdy }}</ref><ref>{{cite web title=Josh Duhamel Biography url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0241049/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm publisher=Internet Movide Database accessdate=1 June 2016}}</ref>

==Career==
Duhamel has stated, "After college, I followed an ex-girlfriend to northern California, did a bunch of odd jobs." He won the title of Male Model of the Year in an International Modeling and Talent Association (IMTA) competition in 1997<ref name=IMTA>{{cite web title=IMTA Alum Josh Duhamel Transforms Into Action Hero publisher=International Modeling and Talent Agency date=July 2, 2007 url=http://www.imta.com/pressDetail.aspx?type=joshduhamel070207 accessdate=July 26, 2017 deadurl=bot: unknown archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20071020125213/http://www.imta.com/pressDetail.aspx?type=joshduhamel070207 archivedate=October 20, 2007 df=mdy-all}}</ref> (the runner-up was actor Ashton Kutcher).<ref name=JoshAshton>{{cite web title=Josh Duhamel: People.com work=People Weekly publisher=Time Inc url=http://www.people.com/people/josh_duhamel accessdate=September 20, 2008}}</ref>

Duhamel began his acting career as an extra (actor) extra in the music videos for Donna Summers "I Will Go With You (Con te partirò)" and Christina Aguileras "Genie in A Bottle" in 1999.<ref name=USAWeekend>{{cite web last=Hatty first=Michele title=Meet up-and-comer Josh Duhamel work=USA Weekend publisher=Gannett Company date=February 6, 2005 url=http://www.usaweekend.com/05_issues/050206/050206josh_duhamel.html archive-url=https://archive.is/20121216130752/http://www.usaweekend.com/05_issues/050206/050206josh_duhamel.html dead-url=yes archive-date=December 16, 2012 accessdate=September 20, 2008}} </ref> Josh trained with [http://www.scottseditaacting.com Scott Sedita Acting Studios]. Later that year, he won the role of Leo du Pres on the American Broadcasting Company ABC soap opera All My Children. His work on the show, especially Leo du Pres and Greenlee Smythe his characters pairing with Greenlee Smythe (portrayed by actor Rebecca Budig), garnered critical acclaim.<ref name="SoapOperaDigest.com">{{cite news title=Best New Couple work=Soap Opera Digest year=2000 accessdate=September 6, 2007}}</ref><ref name="eyeonsoaps.net">{{cite web first=Carolyn  last=Aspenson  title=Redefining the Super Couple  publisher=eyeonsoaps.net  date=February 5, 2009  accessdate=March 5, 2009  url=http://eyeonsoaps.net/wp/?p=1765  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090627112608/http://eyeonsoaps.net/wp/?p=1765  archivedate=June 27, 2009 }}</ref> In 2000, he posed fully nude for Greg Gormans As I See It photography book.

In 2003, he received a Daytime Emmy Award nomination for the Special Fan Award for Americas Favorite Couple in 2002, shared with Budig, and a Daytime Emmy Award for Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series Outstanding Supporting Actor for his portrayal of Leo du Pres.<ref>{{cite web title=The 29th Annual Daytime Emmy Awards work=Soapcentral url=http://www.soapcentral.com/soapcentral/awards/emmys/2001.php accessdate=September 20, 2008}}</ref> Duhamel left All My Children in 2002 to pursue other acting opportunities. In 2003, Duhamel landed a primetime role on the NBC show Las Vegas (TV series) Las Vegas, playing the head of security for the Montecito Casino, Danny McCoy. His character succeeded James Caan (actor) James Caans as head of the casino after Caan departed the series at the end of the fourth season. The series ended in 2008.

Duhamel made his big-screen acting debut in 2004s Win a Date with Tad Hamilton!, then starred in the thriller Turistas (2006). After watching an episode of Las Vegas in which Duhamels character had just returned from the war in Iraq, Steven Spielberg handpicked him for the role of Captain William Lennox in 2007s summer Blockbuster (entertainment) blockbuster film Transformers (film) Transformers<ref name=IMTA/> (a film for which Spielberg was the Executive producer#Motion pictures and television executive producer). Duhamel reprised the role in three of the sequels, Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen, released in June 2009,<ref name=Variety>{{cite web title=Transformers sequel sets 2009 date work=Variety  publisher=Reed Business Information url=http://www.variety.com/article/VR1117972852.html?categoryid=13&cs=1 last=McClintock first=Pamela date=September 26, 2007 accessdate=September 20, 2008}}</ref> Transformers: Dark of the Moon, released in June 2011, and Transformers: The Last Knight, released in June 2017.

Duhamel was the official pace car driver for the 2009 Indianapolis 500, driving the 2010 Chevrolet Camaro Pace Car.<ref name="Josh D">{{cite web url=http://www.indy500.com/news/13760/Actor_Josh_Duhamel_To_Drive_Chevrolet_Camaro_Pace_Car_At_Indy_500  title=Actor Josh Duhamel To Drive Chevrolet Camaro Pace Car At Indy 500  publisher=indy500.com  date=2009-04-30  accessdate=2009-04-30  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120216000727/http://www.indy500.com/news/13760/Actor_Josh_Duhamel_To_Drive_Chevrolet_Camaro_Pace_Car_At_Indy_500  archivedate=February 16, 2012  df= }}</ref> In 2010, he played the lead role in the film When in Rome as Nick Beamon. In 2014 Duhamel starred in the touching drama Youre Not You (2014) playing the husband of Kate (Hilary Swank), who has the degenerative disease ALS.

On March 23, 2013, Duhamel hosted the 2013 Kids Choice Awards, which was broadcast live from Los Angeles, California.<ref>The Associated Press, [http://www.miamiherald.com/2013/01/23/3196090/josh-duhamel-named-host-of-kids.html Josh Duhamel named host of Kids Choice Awards], The Miami Herald, January 23, 2013</ref> He and his co-star from Safe Haven (film) Safe Haven, Julianne Hough, performed a Safe Haven-themed showcase appearance via video on The Price is Right (U.S. game show) The Price is Right with Drew Carey.

In 2018, he was used on a Taco Bell commercial to advertise Mexican spiced fries with nacho cheese sauce called "Web of Fries".

On March 2018, it was announced that Josh Duhamel will be starring alongside Megan Fox, in the independent family comedy “Think Like a Dog.”<ref>{{Cite news url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/megan-fox-josh-duhamel-think-like-a-dog-1202734317/ title=Megan Fox, Josh Duhamel to Reunite in Family Comedy ‘Think Like a Dog’ last=McNary first=Dave date=2018-03-23 work=Variety access-date=2018-04-04 language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news url=http://www.nola.com/tv/index.ssf/2018/03/megan_fox_josh_duhamel_new_orleans.html title=Megan Fox, Josh Duhamel headed for New Orleans and Think Like a Dog work=NOLA.com access-date=2018-04-04 language=en-US}}</ref>
