
James Cunningham (born 1973 or 1974)<ref name=owe>{{cite news url=http://www.canada.com/Generation+should+responsible+kids+financial+literacy/4237680/story.html title=Generation Owe: who should be responsible for kids financial literacy? last=Laucius first=Joanne work=Canada.com date=7 February 2011 accessdate=6 May 2011}}</ref> is a Canadian stand-up comedian and TV host.

He is the host of Food Network (Canada) Food Network Canadas and Cooking Channel USs Eat St., a TV show about North American street food that debuted in 2011. He initially auditioned for another show before being offered the job of hosting Eat St.<ref name=shortorder>{{cite news url=http://blogs.miaminewtimes.com/shortorder/2011/05/eat_streets_james_cunningham_e.php title=Eat Streets James Cunningham Searches for Miami Food Truck Girl last=Doss first=Laine work=Short Order publisher=Miami New Times date=5 May 2011 accessdate=6 May 2011}}</ref>.Other television appearances include "Last Comic Standing" and "Just for Laughs."<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.winnipegfreepress.com/arts-and-life/food/new-series-eat-st-captures-revolution-in-gourmet-food-says-host-119178534.html title=New series Eat St. captures revolution in gourmet food, says host last=Ahearn first=Victoria work=Winnipeg Free Press date=4 April 2011 accessdate=6 May 2011}}</ref> He has also worked as a TV warm up act.<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.canadaeast.com/entertainment/article/537325 title=Shining a spotlight on the crowd warmer, the unsung hero of TV tapings last=Ahearn first=Victoria work=The Canadian Press date=12 January 2009 accessdate=6 May 2011}}</ref>

He wrote and hosts "Funny Money", a stage show designed to teach students about finance.<ref>{{cite news url=http://www.canada.com/ottawacitizen/news/bustech/story.html?id=c6211306-6bf4-4f42-8b2c-5b837d1eeba2  title=Funny money banks laughs  date=26 April 2008  work=Ottawa Citizen  accessdate=6 May 2011  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121110091320/http://www.canada.com/ottawacitizen/news/bustech/story.html?id=c6211306-6bf4-4f42-8b2c-5b837d1eeba2  archivedate=10 November 2012  deadurl=yes  df= }}</ref> He does over 300 stand-up performances a year, mostly "Funny Money".<ref name=crave/>

Cunningham was born in Toronto,<ref name=crave>{{cite news url=http://www.craveonline.com/tv/interviews/167369-james-cunningham-of-qeat-stq-has-the-funny-and-the-food title=Eat St.s James Cunningham last=Cox first=Jennifer work=Crave Online date=4 May 2011 accessdate=6 May 2011}}</ref> where his father was an accountant. He studied drama and minored in finance at the University of Toronto.<ref name=owe/>
