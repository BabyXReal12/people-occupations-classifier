==Early life==
Walken was born Ronald Walken (in tribute to Ronald Colman) on March 31, 1943 in Astoria, Queens, New York City, the son of Rosalie ({{nee Russell}}; May 16, 1907 – March 26, 2010), an immigrant from Glasgow, Scotland, and Paul Wälken (October 5, 1903 – February 23, 2001), an immigrant from Horst, Germany.
His father owned and operated Walkens Bakery in Astoria.
Walken was raised a Methodist.
He and his brothers Kenneth and Glenn were child actors on television in the 1950s, influenced by their mothers dreams of stardom.
