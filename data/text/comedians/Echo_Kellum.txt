
Echo Kellum (born August 29, 1982){{citation needed date=July 2018}} is an American actor and comedian. Kellum is best known for his roles as Mister Terrific (comics) Curtis Holt on The CW drama series Arrow (TV series) Arrow, Tommy on the Fox Broadcasting Company FOX sitcom Ben and Kate,<ref name="dallasnews">{{cite web  author=Robert Wilonsky  url=http://popcultureblog.dallasnews.com/2012/12/echo-kellum-co-star-of-foxs-ben-and-kate-was-in-town-promoting-his-show-this-is-our-visit.html/  title=Echo Kellum, co-star of FOX’s ‘Ben and Kate,’ was in town promoting his show. This is our visit.  work=The Dallas Morning News Pop Culture Blog  publisher=The Dallas Morning News Inc.  date=December 4, 2012  accessdate=2016-04-05}}</ref> and Hunter on NBCs Sean Saves the World.<ref name="NYPost">{{cite web  author=Michael Starr  url=https://nypost.com/2013/10/02/will-grace-vet-best-reason-to-watch-sean-saves-the-world/  title=‘Will & Grace’ vet best reason to watch ‘Sean Saves the World’  work=New York Post  date=October 2, 2013  accessdate=2016-04-05  quote=...and Hayes is nicely supported by Echo Kellum and Megan Hilty as co-workers Hunter (he of the understated sarcasm) and Liz (also Sean’s best friend outside the office).}}</ref>

==Early life ==
Born in Chicago, Illinois,<ref>{{cite news  author=Darel Jevens  url=http://chicago.suntimes.com/entertainment/chicagos-echo-kellum-cast-as-mr-terrific-on-arrow/  title=Chicago’s Echo Kellum cast as Mr. Terrific on ‘Arrow’  work=Chicago Sun-Times  date=July 20, 2015  accessdate=2016-06-02}}</ref> he moved to Los Angeles in late 2009 to pursue a career in comedy.<ref name="UCB">{{cite web url=http://losangeles.ucbtheatre.com/talent/view/21329  title=UCB Theatre • Echo Kellum  publisher=UCB Comedy  date=  accessdate=2012-12-07}}</ref> He is a graduate of The Groundlings, I.O. West, and the Upright Citizens Brigade.<ref name="dallasnews"/><ref name="UCB"/>

==Career==
Kellum gained his first major role in 2012 when he was cast in the FOX comedy Ben and Kate (then titled Ben Fox Is My Manny) playing the role of Tommy, the best friend of the titular character Ben (Nat Faxon).<ref>{{cite web  author=Nellie Andreeva  url=http://deadline.com/2012/02/michael-arden-joins-anger-management-echo-kellum-cast-in-ben-fox-is-my-manny-230758/  title=Michael Arden Joins Anger Management, Echo Kellum Cast In Ben Fox Is My Manny  work=Deadline Hollywood  date=February 14, 2012  accessdate=2016-04-05}}</ref> Following Ben and Kate, Kellum was cast as Hunter in NBCs Sean Saves the World starring Sean Hayes (actor) Sean Hayes and Megan Hilty.

In 2015, Kellum was cast in the recurring role of Curtis Holt, based on the DC Comics DC character Mister Terrific (comics) Mister Terrific, in the fourth season of The CW drama series Arrow (TV series) Arrow.<ref name="EW-Arrow">{{cite web  author=Natalie Abrams  url=http://www.ew.com/article/2015/07/16/arrow-echo-kellum-mr-terrific-season-4  title=Arrow casts Mr. Terrific for season 4  work=Entertainment Weekly  date=July 17, 2015  accessdate=2016-04-05}}</ref> Curtis is a technological savant and inventor who works at Palmer Technologies under the supervision of Felicity Smoak, and later becomes a member of Team Arrow. In April 2016, it was announced that Kellum would be promoted to series regular for the fifth season of Arrow.<ref name="TVL-Arrow">{{cite web  author=Matt Webb Mitovich  url=http://tvline.com/2016/04/04/arrow-season-5-echo-kellum-series-regular-curtis/  title=Arrow Season 5: Echo Kellum Promoted to Series Regular as Curtis  work=TVLine  date=April 4, 2016  accessdate=2016-04-05}}</ref>

In 2017,<ref>http://fanfest.com/2016/07/06/echo-kellum-talks-about-the-future-of-arrows-mister-terrific</ref> Kellum appeared in Netflixs Girlfriends Day<ref>{{cite web  author=Rebecca Ford  url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/netflix-nabs-chris-sparlings-mercy-842494  title=Netflix Nabs Chris Sparlings Mercy, Girlfriends Day With Bob Odenkirk (Exclusive)  work=The Hollywood Reporter  date=November 23, 2015}}</ref> as Madsen, beat poet and a co-worker of Bob Odenkirks character.<ref>http://www.talentmonthly.com/arrows-echo-kellum-talks-playing-curtis-mr-terrific-and-more/</ref>

Kellum voices the character "King Joaquín" on the Disney Channel animated series Elena of Avalor. He also voices multiple characters for the Adult Swim animated series Rick and Morty.
