Natasha Leggero (born March 26, 1974) is an American actress, comedian, and writer. She rose to fame after appearing as the host of MTVs The 70s House (2005), and for her tenure as a regular roundtable panelist on Chelsea Handlers late-night talk show Chelsea Lately.
Her comedy, characterized as observational humor observational, has been noted by critics for its use of satire as commentary on such themes as celebrity culture and class.

Her stand-up television appearances include performances on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno,Â The Late Late Show (CBS TV series) The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson,  Last Call with Carson Daly, The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon and she has appeared on multiple Comedy Central stand-up programs as well as their animated show Ugly Americans (TV series) Ugly Americans, wherein she voiced Callie Maggotbone. 

In 2015, she co-created the period sitcom Another Period for Comedy Central, in which she stars alongside co-creator Riki Lindhome.
