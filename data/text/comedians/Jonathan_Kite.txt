Jonathan Kite (born September 2, 1979) is an Americans American actor, comedian and impressionist.  He is perhaps best known for his role as Oleg Golishevsky on 2 Broke Girls.
==Life and career==
Kite grew up in Skokie, Illinois,
the son of Lynn and David Kite.
He attended Old Orchard Junior High and Niles North High School. He has a theater degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign.
