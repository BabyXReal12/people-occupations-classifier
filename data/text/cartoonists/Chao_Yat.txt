
Chao Yat (草日, born 21 May 1968) is a Hong Kong comics artist,<ref>{{cite web title=About Chao Yat url=http://www.bigbirdstudio.com/chao_about.asp work=bigbirdstudio.com accessdate=3 January 2011 deadurl=yes archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110707233359/http://www.bigbirdstudio.com/chao_about.asp archivedate=7 July 2011 df=}}</ref> his comics first appeared in Ming Pao in 1991.

Subsequent works appeared in Sing Pao, Oriental Daily and Hong Kong Economic Times.

Since then, his published comics include Sams Manhua Series and Leungs Saga Series.
