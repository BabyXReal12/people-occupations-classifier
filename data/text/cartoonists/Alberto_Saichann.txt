Alberto Saichann is a comic book artist and penciller born in Argentina.<ref name=lambiek>{{cite web   title=Alberto Saichann  url=http://lambiek.net/artists/s/saichann_alberto.htm   publisher=Lambiek  accessdate=21 June 2010  archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20100801030045/http://lambiek.net/artists/s/saichann_alberto.htm  archivedate= 1 August 2010 <!--DASHBot-->  deadurl= no}}</ref>

He has worked for Marvel Comics (Punisher: Return to the Big Nothing, Punisher War Zone) and DC Comics (Looney Tunes).
