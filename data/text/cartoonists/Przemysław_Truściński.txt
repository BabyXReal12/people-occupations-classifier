Przemysław Truściński (born 15 December 1970 in Siedlce) Poles Polish comics artist. He graduated Academy of Fine Arts In Łódź. He uses the Trust nickname.<ref name="Gildia">{{cite web   url = http://www.komiks.gildia.pl/tworcy/przemyslaw_truscinski/   title = Przemysław Truściński (Trust)   language = pl   accessdate = December 19, 2013   publisher = Gildia.pl sp. z.o.o.   issn = 2300-004X   work = Gildia.pl}}</ref>

==Biography==
He has worked as a comics artist for Gazeta Wyborcza, Playboy, Machina (magazine) Machina and Newsweek and as an advertisement artist in Pepsi and Nescafe. Awarded several prizes in International Festival of Comics and Games in Łódź. His short stories anthology Trust - historia choroby was published 2003, but numerous other short comics were published in various magazines and anthologies.<ref name="Gildia"/> His work on press comic was gathered in the Komiks W-wa compilation.<ref name="Gildia2">{{cite web   url = http://www.komiks.gildia.pl/komiksy/komiks-w-wa/1   title = Komiks W-wa   language = pl   accessdate = December 19, 2013   publisher = Gildia.pl sp. z.o.o.   issn = 2300-004X   work = Gildia.pl}}</ref>

He is well known for his The Witcher game project.<ref name="Culture">{{cite web   url = http://culture.pl/pl/tworca/przemyslaw-trust-truscinski   title = Przemysław Trust Truściński   work = Culture.pl   author = Łukasz Chmielewski   date = August 2013   language = pl   publisher = Adam Mickiewicz Institute   accessdate = December 19, 2013}}</ref>
