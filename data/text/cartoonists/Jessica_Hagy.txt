Jessica Hagy is known for the online cartoon Indexed (cartoon) Indexed which is a collection of charts and diagrams hand drawn on 3x5 index cards and organized into a blog format. She has compiled her cartoons into a book called Indexed. The cartoon has also appeared in Freakonomics, Plenty (magazine) Plenty, Good (magazine) Good Magazine, and BBC.co.uk.
Indexed was voted by readers on Time.com as the best blog of 2008.
