==Biography==

===Life===
Addams was born in Westfield, New Jersey. Son of Grace M. (Spear) (1879-1960) and Charles Huey Addams (1882-1963), a piano-company executive who had studied to be an architect,
he was known as "something of a rascal around the neighborhood" as childhood friends recalled.
