Jason Craig (born June 26, 1974, Eglin Air Force Base in Niceville, Florida Niceville, FL) is an People of the United States American comic book artist 

==Career==
Jason Craig started his career in Chicago, in 1991, working on and off for various publishers before moving away to pursue a full-time career in advertising. Following a near-fatal automobile accident in 2002, Craig was placed into a coma briefly and spent the following year in physical rehab, after which he returned to Chicago to continue his career in comics.{{Citation needed date=January 2010}}

Craigs most notable work in the comics industry has been for Wildstorm Comics, drawing Freddy vs. Jason vs. Ash and Freddy vs. Jason vs. Ash 2: Nightmare Warriors its sequel.<ref name=RetroSlashers>{{cite web  url=http://retroslashers.net/blog/exclusive-interview-with-freddy-vs-jason-vs-ash-2-artist-jason-craig/  title=Exclusive Interview With Freddy vs. Jason vs. Ash 2 Artist Jason Craig  publisher=RetroSlashers  date=May 24, 2009  accessdate=September 16, 2009  deadurl=yes  archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090628150923/http://retroslashers.net/blog/exclusive-interview-with-freddy-vs-jason-vs-ash-2-artist-jason-craig/  archivedate=June 28, 2009  df= }}</ref>
