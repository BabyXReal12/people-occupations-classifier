Jason Craig (born June 26, 1974, Eglin Air Force Base in Niceville, Florida Niceville, FL) is an People of the United States American comic book artist 

==Career==
Jason Craig started his career in Chicago, in 1991, working on and off for various publishers before moving away to pursue a full-time career in advertising. Following a near-fatal automobile accident in 2002, Craig was placed into a coma briefly and spent the following year in physical rehab, after which he returned to Chicago to continue his career in comics.{{Citation needed date=January 2010}}

Craigs most notable work in the comics industry has been for Wildstorm Comics, drawing Freddy vs. Jason vs. Ash and Freddy vs. Jason vs. Ash 2: Nightmare Warriors its sequel.
Craig drew the reboot issues of the comic series Evil Ernie, written by Jesse Blaze Snider. The first issue was released in October 2012.
