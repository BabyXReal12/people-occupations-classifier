Martin Handford (born 27 September 1956)
is an English childrens author and illustrator who gained worldwide fame in the mid-1980s with his Wheres Wally? creation (known as Wheres Waldo? in North America).

==Early life==
Born in Hampstead, London, Handford was a solitary child, born to divorced parents. He began drawing crowds when he was 4 or 5 years old, and later as a child, making stick figures on paper.
